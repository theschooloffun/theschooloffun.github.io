Glasses, Glasses on My Nose!

Jul 29, 2010

Two third of our household (that makes my husband and I) are wearing glasses. So, I guess it was no surprise that the little guy was so into glasses.

While I wouldn’t say that it’s a totally good thing (judging from the number of times he threw and brake our glasses), I say it calls for a fun project  together.

What we had:

  1. cardboard

  2. black marker

  3. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

First, I drew a pair of glasses on the cardboard then cut along the line.

Drawing the pattern

Fold on the left. And on the right. There you go, the glasses are ready!

Ready to wear!

Too bad, the little guy couldn’t really participate. Still, he was excited enough to be the model. He was all giggling when I put the glasses on him!

Trying on the new glasses
