Aug 11, 2010

‘Twas probably the season of dragon fruits! And thanks to a certain someone, we suddenly had lots of dragon fruits at home.

The exotic look seemed to beckon the little guy’s curiosity. No matter where I put the dragon fruits, as long as he could still see them, he would try to reach for these fruits.

So, out we took our truck loader and pail. Let us show you how dragon fruits could be lotsa fun!
