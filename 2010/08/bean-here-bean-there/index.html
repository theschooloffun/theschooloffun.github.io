Bean Here, Bean There

Aug 4, 2010

It’s hard to get anything done in the kitchen if you have a tail as busy as my little guy. So, here’s what we did to keep him occupied in a safe place.

What we had:

  1. mung beans (kidney beans are equally fun!)

  2. plastic container

  3. metal container

Red flag:

I made sure I was there with the little guy for the first few minutes. I didn’t want him to swallow any of the beans or to put inside his nose or ears. I only stepped away from him once I was sure that he had no interest to put the beans inside any parts of his body.

Let the fun begin!

Basically, we just put some water inside the plastic container. I threw in some beans and let his little hands pick the beans. Once picked, I got him to put the beans inside the metal container._ Clang! Clang! _Oh, how we loved the sound of it!
