Box 'Em!

Aug 18, 2010

Obviously, the little guy still hasn’t got over his new hobby of putting things into any box. When he saw me carrying an empty tissue box, he quickly snatched it. He shook the box, and took a peek inside. Then, he looked around, as if looking for something small enough to stuff his new box.

I gave him some clothes pegs and he was straight away one happy chap!

To box or not to box...

He was so excited about this whole boxing things that he started looking at the possibility of putting himself into the box, too!

Robo-box in Action!

It was kinda cute when he walked around with the box on one of his feet. He also loved the sound when he stomp his foot, together with the box.

Now I’m tempted to get him another box so that he could have similar boxes on both feet. Can’t wait to finish another box of tissue!!
