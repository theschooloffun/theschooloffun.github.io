Eencey Weencey Spidey

Aug 28, 2010

Lately, the little guy had been asking for the markers. Of course, such episodes usually ended up with some meltdowns. I know if I let him have the markers, something is bound to be stained. That is why I rarely let him play with markers, unless I’m 100% confident that I would be next to him, watching every movement he makes with each marker.

Like when we made this spidey…

What we had:

  1. egg cartons

  2. white paper (wiggle eyes work great, too!)

  3. markers

  4. black wool thread

  5. glue

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we cut out an egg carton. Only then I quietly handed the marker to the little guy. I kept a watchful eye, while praying that he would not stain anything.

Preparing his ammunition...

A few minutes passed by… he had diligently completed the coloring part. He made some scribbles on a cardboard, but nowhere else… pheww…

Almost done!

He continued scribbling when I was busy putting on the eyes and legs.

Hullo, Spidey!

He scribbled quite a good deal on the cardboard. See what we could even make out of that cardboard!

Psst... I'm undercover!
