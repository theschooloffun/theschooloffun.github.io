Graffiti Art by Yours Truly

And, no, it wasn’t a piece of paper. It was our WALL.

Lesson learned: Never ever leave a toddler with a crayon!

_I’m so glad they have invented washable crayons!_

Can’t imagine if he used marker, instead! Or maybe that would be the easiest and fastest way to get my husband to repaint the whole house. Hee hee.. _grinz_
