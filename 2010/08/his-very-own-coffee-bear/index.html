His Very Own Coffee Bear

Aug 23, 2010

Look what we found! This was probably the very first craft he made.

It was very simple, even for a 14-month old. Just give him a paper plate with a picture. We chose bear. Paint the bear with glue and let him sprinkle some coffee powder onto the plate. Teach him how to shake the powder inside the plate, too. It would surely get him to giggle! Let it dry and pat any excess.

Voila! A coffee bear with nice smell and texture!
