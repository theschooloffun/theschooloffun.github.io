It's Magic! Pepper Magic!

Aug 26, 2010

We decided to try out this Pepper and Dish Soap Experiment. After reading that entry, it was just so hard to resist trying it out. I know the little guy would love it. As long as it involves water, he would definitely love to watch what I would be doing with full attention!

The fact that my little guy is not even 2 years old makes my life easier. At least for now, I have a valid reason why I could skip the part about explaining the science behind. Hee hee…

So… there we were, in the living room, ready to get wet and peppery. I let the little guy use our pepper grinder. He was so excited about it. He kept twisting and twisting, spreading pepper all over the place. Both of us sneezed a few times…

Twisting Left and Right

And when he finally got to dip his fingers and watch how the pepper floated away, he went “Wowwww…” with his cutest voice.

Magic Fingers!

Hahaha… he must be impressed with himself! No doubt. I was very impressed, myself! I wish  they taught all this stuff back in my school days… I would probably have been a more diligent student!
