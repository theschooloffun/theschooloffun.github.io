Race Me, If You Dare...

Aug 28, 2010

It actually made a difference when you have a husband who appreciates the little kid inside you.

Like my husband.

He called it “creativity”. I called it…well, having fun and sharing the fun with the little guy.

Anyway, ever since he noticed that I was into cardboard, he brought home some from his office. He also made sure that whenever he opened any package, he kept the cardboard intact.

Like this keyboard box, which I turned into a racing track… :)

Vroom... vroom...

You bet, the little guy loves it! He even loves the corner parking space the most. And to think that I almost cut that part out! Hee hee… It was initially the compartment for the keyboard’s USB receiver, I think… Just add a door, and it nicely changed into a parking space… :)

His Fave Part: Corner Parking Space

Now, if you could just excuse me, I need to figure out what to do with the rest of the card boards… :S Can’t risk to have the house turn into a junk yard!
