Turtlely Awesome Egg Carton!

Aug 18, 2010

I still remember how I was grinning ear to ear after my trip to Ya Kun Kaya Toast the other day. The generous lady at the counter granted my wish of having egg cartons, without the eggs! It was like winning a prize! You see, I’ve been wanting to make something from egg cartons, but the thought of having to buy a lot of eggs always scares me! I do bake occasionally. Yet, unless I have to feed half a dozen people or more, I don’t think I would ever finish the eggs fast enough!

Anyway, I brought home 2 egg cartons. I was so excited that it took me quite some time to decide which one I would want to make first. In the end, I blindly took one paint out from the box. I’ve got myself green paint. Ha! It wasn’t difficult to decide then! A turtle we should make!

What we had:

  1. egg carton

  2. paper (for the limbs, we chose orange)

  3. scissors

  4. glue (you could also use clear tapes)

  5. markers

  6. paint brush

  7. paint

Let the Fun Begin!

Getting ready

First, we cut out one egg carton.

One egg carton...

Next, I let the little guy painted it. He used the brush, but after a while, he decided to use his fingers instead. Still, he was perfectly happy with what he was doing. He even looked proud of his artistic achievement!

Go, Green!

The last touch was to add the head and four limbs. We used orange paper for all. I helped him draw the eyes and mouth, too. See how cute it looks!

Turtlely Awesome, ain't I!
