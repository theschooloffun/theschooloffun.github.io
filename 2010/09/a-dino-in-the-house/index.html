A Dino in the House!

Sep 9, 2010

There’s a dinosaur in our living room!

Yup, we thought dinosaurs went extinct years ago. But, now we have one in our living room.

It all started when we finally managed to collect 2 empty tissue boxes.

Let us bring you through the details.. :)

What we had:

  1. two empty tissue boxes

  2. paper (we used brochures, which happened to be green - perfect color!)

  3. markers

  4. glue

  5. scissors

Hatching Equipment

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we glued a piece of paper underneath one tissue box. We then drew a dinosaur’s footprint. A BIG one!

Dino's Footprint

We then made another dino’s footprint using the other tissue box and another piece of paper.

Next, we cut the footprints out and look what we’ve got!

A Pair of Feet!

The best part was that we didn’t need to wait for months or years for our little dino. And when he finally cracked out of his eggs, my, how he roared and stomped!

Roarr! Beware of the Little Dino!
