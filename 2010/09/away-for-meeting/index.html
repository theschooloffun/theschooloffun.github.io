Away for Meeting

Sep 20, 2010

The folding chairs are not in the usual place.

My soft toys are missing.

…

It’s time for Detective Mommy to investigate. The little guy must be up to something!

Something is brewing...

Yup, he surely was up to something!

Soft Toys Meeting: Evidence #1

Hehehe… I particularly like the way he put the duck in the middle, but in upright position. At first, when he went to put the duck in the middle, it looked as if all the other soft toys were waiting for their meal of the day: roast duck… :P But then, he insisted on getting the duck to ‘sit’ in upright position. It didn’t look that bad when he managed to get the duck to sit properly… :) See, all the soft toys were just meeting up to have a tea time together… :)

Detective Mommy shall retire then… ;)
