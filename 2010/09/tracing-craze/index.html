Here’s the latest craze in our home:

Tracing!

Yup! Tracing is the newest craze. We trace almost anything we could find. Cookie cutters, lids, and the little guy’s favorite: his own hands!

Hands and Lid

Tracing his hands would make him giggling. Non stop.. :D
