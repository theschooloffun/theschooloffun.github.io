While He was Down with Fever...

Sep 27, 2010

The poor little guy was down with mild fever yesterday… :(

Although the fever was kinda expected, due to his booster jab the day before, it was still awfully weird … and heart-breaking to see him so quiet. Just imagine if you have a toddler who is usually like a little tornado in the house, but suddenly did not want to stay anywhere too far away from his bed. Sleeping. Or simply staring at you.

My day was peaceful, but it was just too… quiet.

But it wasn’t an unproductive day… :) We had a short finger-painting session after lunch… ;)
