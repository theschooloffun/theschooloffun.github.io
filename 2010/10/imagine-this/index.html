Imagine This

Oct 1, 2010

Mommy: Look! An Ant! _pointing to an ant on the floor_

Ant: !!!

The little guy: Smash! Smash! _smashing the floor blindly_

Ant: — _this would be the part where we usually have the long annoying beep in a hospital scene on TV with a dying man_

The little guy: _smiling cheekily_ Wheeeere?

Mommy: _speechless_

And no! This isn’t a part of the syllabus for our School of Fun!
