Lots of Helping Hands

Oct 6, 2010

It was raining this morning. Yet, contrary to popular belief, we were still pretty productive. Or maybe today was just an exception… :) After all, it is hard to resist not to make anything nice looking at those stacks of colorful “hands” that daddy meticulously drew and cut for us the other night. We really appreciate his helping hands!

Lots of hands!

Let the Fun Begin!
