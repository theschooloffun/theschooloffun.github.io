Monsters in the House!

Oct 13, 2010

Who would have ever thought of keeping these unassuming items for weeks?

A treasure or a hoard?

Me. Yes, me!

Call me a hoarder, I’ve been keeping these clips for the past few weeks. Mind you, getting one requires me to wait for a week! And sometimes, when it takes us more than a week to finish the whole bread, it feels like a long wait! Even worse, when the little guy decides to play with the clip and break it, we’ll have to wait for another week… :’(

So, yup… I gave up after collecting two. I thought I’ve collected more than two, but I simply couldn’t find the rest.

What we had:

  1. drawing paper

  2. glue

  3. clips

  4. markers

Let the Fun Begin!

I thought the opening side of the clip would make a great monster’s mouth. So, I got the little guy to put some glue on the side with the price tag.

Unfortunately, in the end I had to take over. Apparently, the little guy was more interested in poking the clip’s  “mouth” with the glue stick. I certainly couldn’t afford to lose any of these two precious clips! Not if that means I have to wait for another week to get the replacement!

No wonder it's called a glue stick

Next, we pasted the clips on a piece of drawing paper. Add the eyes, hairs, etc. The little guy doodled something that looked like a shell of a snail. So, I added antennas for the “snail”.

Now, scream at the top of your lungs, people! We’ve got monsters in the house!
