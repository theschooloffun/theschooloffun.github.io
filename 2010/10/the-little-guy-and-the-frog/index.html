The Little Guy and the Frog

Oct 26, 2010

It’s time to bring out the egg cartons again. They’ve been sitting in the corner, collecting dust.

Since he got pretty excited the last time we went to see some frogs at the supermarket, here’s what we made after we brushed the egg cartons clean… :)

What we had:

  1. egg cartons

  2. paint

  3. paint brush

  4. marker

  5. white, red and green papers (for the limbs, eyes and tongue)

  6. glue

Getting the magic potion ready

Let the Fun Begin!

After cutting one egg carton out, I gave the little guy the honor to paint it green. The little guy obviously had invented a novel way to paint it and himself green at the same time. He put the egg carton on his lap and started swinging his brush back and forth between the egg carton and his clothes. Brilliant!

Scene 1: The Day He Turned Himself Green

Then, when the little guy went to sleep for the night, his Fairy Godmother placed the eyes,  limbs and tongue. And swoosh-swoosh-swish… tadaaaa… the frog is ready to be kissed first thing in the morning!

Scene 2: Ribbit!

Can’t help to feel glad that we painted it green… Otherwise, it’ll look like a flattened lizard… hee heee…
