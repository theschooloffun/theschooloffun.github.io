The Little Guy's Guide to the Halloween

Oct 28, 2010

It’s Halloween time! And there’s no better way to entertain a toddler than a dress-up game! The toddler got to play make believe in the most interesting way, while the mommy got tons of photos to laugh at… _grinz_

It didn’t start with a breeze, though. The futile attempt to dress him like a mummy using toilet paper turned him unfriendly at first. Until he saw his own face with the monster hat… he became very cooperative! He even tried to dress up himself. I guess he started to think that whatever his mommy was doing, she was definitely up to do something exciting with him!

The Shy Red Devil

Well, we surely did get to do lots of exciting things! Check out what other costumes we had!

The Back-to-School/Back-to-Work Scarecrow

They said school and office could be pretty horrifying. So, the little guy wanted to be the hero who would flap his arms to scare the ghosts, including all the evil bullies… :)

The Scarecrow Saves the Day!

Impressed? You know who to call then if you’re dealing with one at work or school!

Supertot

What? A hero needs a cape? No problem. Everything’s under control. We’ve got the cape and the Supertot is ready to take off. We’ve got to warn you, though: do not try this at home! Especially not without anything to cushion the superhero-wanna-be… you never know when and where he would jump!

Supertot, ready to take off

Mickey Mouse

Okie, enough with the superheroes. The world doesn’t need another superhero.  The world needs to giggle more, so the little guy turn himself into a… cute Mickey Mouse!


Sing with me: M-I-C-K-E-Y M-O-U-S-E!

The Uke Player

Speaking about singing and music, what do you think about dressing smart as an ukulele player? Who knows, he might have what it takes to be the next Jake Shimabukuro.

Oh, by the way, this was probably the most terrifying scene during the game. I watched in horror as he plucked the strings with all his might and main! :S

The Uke Player in Action

The Little Green Riding Hood

Move aside, Little Red Riding Hood! Green is the new red! Oops… no matching shoes? It’s okay, we’ll make do with mommy’s turquoise socks… :) And nope, we don’t need that basket to bring food for the grandmother in the fairy tale. Let’s just sing for grandmother. She’ll recover soon when she hears the little guy sing… ;)

The Little Green Riding Hood's Strum n Drum Session

That’s all, folks!

And guess what we got at the end of our game?
