Confessions of a Hoarder

Nov 6, 2010

It really IS happening. I can’t say I haven’t been warned. I’m turning into a hoarder soon… >_<

It usually starts with spending a few minutes at the weekly thrift store and it ends with me, going home with something on my hands (or on my husband’s hands)…

Like that weekend when we brought home an old typewriter.

Or like those many weekends we brought home stacks of children books… :S

Or like a few weeks ago when we brought home something called Fun-ominoes. The colors were so striking that I made up my mind within seconds! I didn’t even bother to check whether the set was still complete. Well.. who cares, anyway. I don’t think we’ll be playing it for real, or at least, not for the time being. Hee hee…

And indeed, it was a good investment. We’re really grateful to whoever left that thing at the thrift store… 0:) What is supposed to be a kind of domino games has made bath time even more fun! The little guy loves fitting them all together, just like puzzle pieces… :)

Tiling up the pieces...

Aww… I don’t feel so bad about buying it anymore… _grinz_ :P Now, if only I could find more space to store all these… _look right, look left_
