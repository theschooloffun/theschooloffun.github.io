Start Your Engine, Gentlemen!

Nov 3, 2010

What would cross your mind when you have all these?

Fun supply

Check out what we made out of those… ;)

What we had:

  1. kitchen towel rolls

  2. aluminium foil

  3. sticky tape or glue

  4. cardboard

  5. yarn

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we wrapped both kitchen towel rolls with the aluminium foil.

All wrapped

Then, we pasted cardboard underneath, placing the two rolls next to each other. Actually, we could just stick the two rolls together using glue or sticky tape. But, we thought having the cardboard would make it easier to put on the yarn later on.

Putting on the base

Speaking about the yarn… While I was away to find the yarn, the little guy decided to get busy, too..

Making Waves

Geee, I should have known it! I shouldn’t have left him with the aluminium foil… >_< Great, now I have tons of aluminium foil for more art&craft sessions… :S

Anyway, check out what we’ve got… ;)

It's a pair of tanks... it's.. a rocket!

And so the little guy zoomed left and right with his new rocket. No battery needed. Hahaha…

I thought of cutting some red, orange and yellow papers to paste at the bottom of the rocket. You know, just to make it look more real… :P Too bad, I ran out of battery myself… down with mild fever… :S So I just sat on the couch, watching my little guy playing with his new engine… :) He really was one happy little chap!
