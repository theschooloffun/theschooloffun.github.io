Car Wash

Dec 8, 2010

There’s nothing new about this. Every time anyone in the house is down with flu, I make sure the little guy’s toys are “professionally cleaned” … :) No, we don’t send the toys anywhere to be cleaned. That’s just another way to say that the toys are to be properly sanitized to ensure the flu germs don’t spread to anyone… ;)

Usually, I would clean his toys when he has fallen asleep. Yet, this time round, the little guy offered his help. The weather was great, so I let him help out.

First, soak the towel..

Then, squeeze...

And, wipe!

He got really excited about this whole car wash thing! I think we should do this more often… :D
