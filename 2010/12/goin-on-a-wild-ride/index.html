Goin' on a Wild Ride!

Dec 17, 2010

This is just the perfect thing to make when feeling lazy to do anything too fancy… ;) I especially love the fact that it looks very nice in photos. Hahaha… :D Hey, it almost even looks like a real expensive toy set! Just check it out yourself!

What we had:

  1. drawing papers (card boards would be better, actually)

  2. cloth tape (we used grey cloth tape)

  3. masking tape (you could use white paper, too!)

  4. scissors

  5. toy cars

  6. wooden blocks or lego

Let the Fun Begin!

To say that it’s as easy as 1, 2, 3 would be an overstatement. There is only one step! All you need to do is just to tag the drawing paper with the cloth tape and you’ll get one straight road. Add some masking tapes and you’ll get the road divider… ;)

Make more and you’ll get one whole track for the little one to play with his toy cars. Ha! Told you it’s easy to make!

A busy, yet peaceful street

Uh-oh... there's a police car!

You could use wooden blocks or lego to make the surrounding buildings. The more colorful, the more interesting… :) If you have some tree figurines or even dinosaur figurines, you could add them in, too! It would be just as fun as creating a Jurassic Park scene! Hee hee… :P

But, who needs dinosaurs to make a Jurassic Park scene if you have one little godzilla like mine going on a wild ride in the house? Hee heee… :P
