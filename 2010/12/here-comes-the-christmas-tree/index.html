Here Comes the Christmas Tree!

Dec 9, 2010

Listening to Andy Williams’ song “It’s the Most Wonderful Time of the Year”, we just couldn’t agree more with him. Christmas really is the most wonderful time… :)

All of a sudden, we have tons of craft ideas. There are so many ideas that we sometimes feel lost if we don’t quickly jot them down somewhere! We could easily blame the holiday atmosphere (aka: no pressing deadlines looming…:P) for this sudden idea explosion. Yet, we’d rather say that it is simply because Christmas is coming. It’s hard not to feel excited when Christmas is just a few days away… :)

Now that we’ve introduced Santa Claus to the little guy, Christmas tree is the next one in the list.

What we had:

  1. card boards (we used dark green and light green)

  2. white paper (for template, optional)

  3. stickers (lots of them!)

  4. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we drew half the Christmas tree on a folded white paper. We then cut along the line and voila, we had our template ready… :) We then used the template to get two identical trees using the dark green and light green card boards.

Let's rock around the X'mas Trees!

The next part was the little guy’s favorite: pasting stickers all over the Christmas trees… :)

Decorating the Christmas Tree

And here’s the final part: we cut a slit along the bottom half on one Christmas tree and along the top half on the other one. We slipped the two trees together along the slits and… tadaaa…. our very own Christmas tree!

O Christmas Tree...

We could have added a yellow star to put on top of the tree.But, let’s just keep that for tomorrow’s activity… :)

As for now… Okie, aren’t we all ready to sing the Christmas songs around the Christmas tree now?! ;)
