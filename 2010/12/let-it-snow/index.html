Let It Snow!

Dec 23, 2010

I realized it wasn’t so great an idea to get a silver tinsel after all. The color just doesn’t make it stand out. In fact, it looks rather dull on its own.

It certainly calls for something else to make it look nicer… :) So, there I went trying to recall something that I learned back in primary school: paper snowflakes!

I love to see how this:

The Making of Red Snowflake

could turn into one of these:

Colorful snowflakes

I also love the fact that each is unique. I always find it hard to make two with the same patterns… :P

The little guy wanted to help out, but he couldn’t do much since this whole thing requires a pair of scissors. Instead, it was fun ‘snow’ games for him… :D

Falling snow!

He also enjoyed throwing paper ‘snowball’, which was basically just a crumpled piece of paper, around while waiting for me putting the snowflakes up.
