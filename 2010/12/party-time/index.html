Party Time!

Dec 13, 2010

It was party time for the little guy last Saturday. We invited some of his friends for a finger painting session at home. Yup, you could imagine how noisy and messy it was! Just put together 11 babies/toddlers in one room and you’ll get the idea!

I think I was more excited than the little guy when it came to the preparation… :P

Crabby plate on the wall

Smiley sun on the wall

Crabby and Octopus Sausages on the Buffet Spread

Nope, the little guy didn’t really paint, either. He was more interested in taking pictures of his friends busy painting. And when the artworks were up to dry, he went one around to check on them. He really acted like an art expert, ready to give his critiques for each piece… :P

Point... point...

Anyway, I just love to see the lines of artworks! I love how each toddler took many different colors and used many different tools to paint, from brush, balls to cars.

Excavator painting

The first batch of artworks

The mess...

Of course, the little guy felt it wouldn’t be fun if he didn’t invite his friends to climb the window and wave bye-bye to the trains… :P

Bye, Choo-choo!

At the end of the party, each little artist brought home his or her own art work, leaving the wall empty (and white) once again. Each of them also brought home the clothes peg used to hang the artwork. Not just another clothes peg, of course… ;)

Honey Stars Butterfly Clothes Peg

The little guy surely had a great time at the party… :D I hope his friends did, too!
