Rock 'n Roll Beads

Dec 25, 2010

This is currently one of his favorite toys.

A truckload of beads

Even after he found the beads coming off, he still insists on playing with the beads. With a chopstick… :)

Chopstick and Beads

Of course, the more beads, the merrier! :)

What we had:

  1. newspapers (cut into strips)

  2. glue

  3. scissors or cutter

  4. paints** **

  5. paintbrush

  6. drawing paper

Getting ready for festive fun beads

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we made the newspaper strips into quills. The little guy helped with the glue.

Glue here...

(Still) Boring Beads

Then, it was up to the little guy, his brush and the paints to turn the beads into festive fun beads… :P Basically, I just let him roll the beads in puddles of colors using a paintbrush. After a while, he also used his fingers.


Rock n Roll inspiration

He wasn’t that patient to wait for the beads to dry. So, I took one piece of drawing paper to let him roll the beads on to dry. Lo and behold! We just created an abstract painting to admire!

Abstract Bead Painting

Of course, the more colors we used, the more abstract our beads became…

A pile of paper beads

There you go, we have a lot of beads to play with now! We could thread the beads using all sorts of things, from chopstick, yarns to even pens!

A String of Fun

We also have an abstract painting to show off at the end… :)

Absolutely Abstract

And guess what we used the painting for… _grinz_

Making a list and checkin' it twice...

Yup, gift tags! Nice… :) At least it doesn’t get wasted… ;)
