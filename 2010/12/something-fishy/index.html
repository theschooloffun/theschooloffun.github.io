Something Fishy

Dec 31, 2010

Obviously, after this incident, we’ve got plenty of aluminium foil to use. We also have quite a number of paper plates from various parties we threw during the year. We certainly ought to make something fun… ;)

What we had:

  1. aluminium foil

  2. glue

  3. paper plate

  4. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

What we were about to make was certainly very easy to make. Yet, it took us about 2 days to complete… :P

First, we prepared the ‘fish’ from the paper plate.


The (not quite) fish bone

Next was the little guy’s part: sticking the scales.

Oeey Gloooey glue

For the scales, we used small pieces of aluminium foil. At first, I actually bothered to cut them nicely, in the shape of real fish scales. Then, I realised, the little guy was just happy tearing the aluminium foil on his own. Haha… which kid doesn’t like to tear anyway?

'Dressing' the fish up

When I saw him playing with the glue instead of sticking the scales, I called it a day. We continued sticking the scales a few days later, when, to him, it looked like a novel thing to do once again… :P

And finally, the fish is ready to be served…. _grinz_
