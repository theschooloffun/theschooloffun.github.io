A Cat on a Roll

Jan 17, 2011

Once again, it was the time of the year when we had accumulated simply too many toilet paper rolls. So, yup, shout it out: craft time!

What we had:

  1. toilet paper roll

  2. paint

  3. marker

  4. paint brush

Let the Fun Begin!

We wanted to make a cat, so we first folded the top part of the roll to make the two pointy ears.

Rollin' Fun!

Next was the little guy’s turn: painting. I let him dip the brush into the bottle since there was not much paint left anyway. It certainly stroke a novelty!

Brush.. brush..

You’d probably need to squint your eyes a bit, but you’d notice that we put clear tapes around the roll. We put there for a reason, of course… :) Since, we were into pets lately, we decided that this cat, too, should have a collar… just like any other pets in our neighborhood! _grinz_

The final part was to draw the eyes, mouth, etc.

A self-proclaimed photographer of the day, the little guy positioned the cat for him to take a picture.

Ready, 1, 2....Cheese!

Haha… okie, obviously, he wasn’t taking a photo of the cat in the above picture… :P Check out what was at the back of the roll… ;)

Okie, Mr. Cat... It's your turn now!

Yup, we drew something else at the back! Thought it would turn out like a hamster or guinea pig, but … too bad, the drawing wasn’t that good… :P We couldn’t even tell what it is now. Hee hee…
