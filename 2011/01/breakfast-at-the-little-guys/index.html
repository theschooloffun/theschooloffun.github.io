Breakfast at the Little Guy's

Jan 19, 2011

The little guy: Mommy… mommy… where?

Where?

Hiding behind the bears, he asked me to find him… :P

Here!

He just invented another version of the peek-a-boo game… Peek-a-Bear! Hee hee… :P Bringing the bears along for breakfast seems like a great idea after all… well, let’s just pray that no jam or chocolate spread would land on their soft fur.

Obviously, he did this peek-a-bear like a thousand times… giving me a chance to take a proper picture for each scene… :P

Trust me, it’s just hard not to laugh at his antics!
