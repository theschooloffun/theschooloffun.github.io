Little Bunny Foo Foo!

Jan 22, 2011

The cat is out… so it’s time for the bunny to hop in.

Dreamy Bunny

It was pretty much similar to how we made our cat, except that we added some cotton wool for the floppy ears and a small cotton ball for the bushy tail. And finally, the green strips of paper were meant to be like curly grass, but they were obviously not long enough to be curly… :P oh well, we were just trying to make full use of some scrap paper.

The cute, dreamy eyes were courtesy of my husband.

In short, the bunny is as sweet as it can be. The little guy, however, was so inspired by the Little Bunny Foo Foo poem… >_<

_Little bunny Foo Foo_
_Hopping on the table_

Scooping up the cars...

And bopping them on the head...

Tsk tsk… naughty bunny!
