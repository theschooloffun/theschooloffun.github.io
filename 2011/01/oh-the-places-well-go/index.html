Oh, The Places We'll Go!

Jan 28, 2011

In case you’re wondering, yup… that title was inspired by one of our favorite Dr Seuss’ books… :)

During one of our downtime days (read: no-Thomas and Friends-and-no-Chuggington days), we decided to go places, too.

In no time, we issued a special passport for the little guy…. :P

Brand New Passport

Next, I  let the little guy pack some of his clothes into a small suitcase. Gee… didn’t he just love those clothes-throwing! He nearly threw all the clothes from his drawer! He only stopped when I decided that it wasn’t a great idea after all and kept all his clothes back in the drawer… :P

All my bags are packed... I'm ready to go

On the door, I put up one big map (one of the loots from the thrift store after realizing that the little guy loves maps… any map will do!). Being a true adventurer, he happily pointed Africa as the first place he wanted to explore… :P

A-fee-caaa...

At the immigration counter, he took out his passport from his bag to get it stamped.

Here's the passport...

PeeWee the Bear was on duty at the immigration counter. Obviously, the little guy thought that PeeWee the Bear had the most boring job: sitting and stamping passports. So, he made an attempt to entertain PeeWee the Bear with his fave car.

Vroom...See?

Yet, after a while, he found out that stamping _was _indeed fun … :P He then took over PeeWee’s duty… :P

Stamp here!

He also realised that PeeWee was just too cute to leave behind. So, he packed PeeWee inside his suitcase… :P

Make yourself comfortable, PeeWee

And off he went again to find another place to explore… with PeeWee, of course… hee hee…

Let's check the map again

And yup, so far, we’ve been to Africa, Asia and South America… _grinz_
