The First Car Wreck in 2011

Jan 4, 2011

Literally broken into pieces...

Barely surviving its first week, this car wreck certainly beats the 2010 record.

I’d given up trying to fix it. The little guy kept dismantling it after I’d got it fixed. Each time, it got even worse! The last time I tried to fix it, I found that the whole thing couldn’t be assembled properly anymore. There must be some broken pieces somewhere.

That makes me think: Maybe he didn’t mean to break it… perhaps he was just curious how this tiny thing was assembled before it reached him… :P

Anyway… I don’t think we could make any vroom-vroom painting with this one… :P Gotta think of something else!
