There's Something about Thomas

Jan 6, 2011

There really is something about Thomas and Friends that makes kids nowadays love everything about it.

Just when I thought the little guy would never stray from his favorite toys (i.e. cars and trucks), he started shouting “Thomas” every now and then. It could be:

a. he wants to watch Thomas and Friends on TV or on YouTube
b. he sees something with Thomas or any other cute train characters (yup, Chuggington characters included)

And here’s the little guy happily flashing his new favorite shorts (well, sort of).

Thomas? Where?

Yup, he loves it very much, except for the fact that the picture is at the back, rather than in front. He must be wondering, if it is his favorite, why does mommy have to put the picture at the back for others, and not for him, to enjoy?

And so… he keeps on trying to take a peek, even if it requires him to stretch his neck… :P

Still, to him, nothing beats steaming around the house on a mommy-powered ‘train’ while taking pictures of the beautiful scenery… And, oh.. Frosty Bear should come along, too! _grinz_

All aboard!
