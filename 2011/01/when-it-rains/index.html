When It Rained...

Jan 4, 2011

Rain on the green grass,
_ and rain on the tree,_
_ rain on the house-top,_
_ but not on me._

Yesterday, it was raining nearly the whole afternoon. Fortunately, we were at home… :) We certainly kept ourselves busy!

Brush & Strainer Painting

What we had:

  1. toothbrush

  2. strainer

  3. paint

  4. water (to dilute the paint)

  5. papers

Getting ready

Let the Fun Begin!

First, I mixed the paint with some water. That way, it was easier to ‘spray’ on using the strainer and the brush.

I’ve been faithfully keeping some old toothbrush. So, we’ve got one baby toothbrush and one adult toothbrush. That way, the little guy could use one, while I, too could play with the other one. Yet, if you think that the little guy would be contented with the baby toothbrush, ha! ha! He took over the adult toothbrush the moment he saw that it made a BIGGER splash on the paper… :P

More often than not, he took both brushes!

Anyway, nothing fancy here… All we had to do was just brush! :)

We’ve Got Nuts!

Clothes Guessing

What we had:

  1. lots of clothes on a drying rack

Let the Fun Begin!

The rain really turned out to be a blessing in disguise, even though it meant that we had to dry our clothes inside… :)

The little guy had a great time pointing whose clothes, which clothes. He got better and better each time… :)

Daddy's... this is daddy's...
