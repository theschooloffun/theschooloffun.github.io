Another One Down...

Feb 28, 2011

It seems that we’ve been passing the flu bugs to each other. Last night, it was my husband’s turn to be down with fever.

Sad. I know…

But, one seemed to be particularly excited about it… :P

Here comes the doctor!

Not sure why… but he looked so happy after he took his daddy’s temperature. Probably his thought was something around “Yayyy… he’s gonna qualify for a sickie and I can bug him for the WHOLE day!”

Whopee! More than 38!

I was easy to tell that the little guy got really excited about poking his daddy and getting him to drink the medicine, too.

Dun worry... it's not gonna hurt...

He then got himself busy trying to write a prescription for his daddy. He almost even burned the midnight oil for it! Thankfully, he fell asleep before the clock struck twelve…

I ... must... stay... awake...zzzz

Me? I got to play the nurse. And considering what the nurse gets, I really don’t mind taking that role… :)
