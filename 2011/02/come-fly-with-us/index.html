Come Fly with Us!

Feb 11, 2011

This was what was left from our castle, after being trampled and sat on by the little guy one too many times…

The Last Survivor of the Wreck-the-Castle Game

I know… very sad looking…

Yet, since the little guy was into airplane recently, I decided to reuse the cardboard to make an airplane.

We were kinda excited at first. We made the body in no time.

An Easy First Step

The little guy also got himself busy painting.


Brush… brush… till it turns into an airplane!

Then, we left it to dry… and nearly a month passed!

We neglected it for almost a month!

The Forgotten Plane

Then, one day, the little guy’s godfather prompted us to watch these airport miniatures.

The little guy went “Wowww…!” the moment he watched the clip and from then on, he flew his trucks, cars and trains and called them “Airplane!”

Hee hee… so, before his vocabulary goes down into just one word, i.e. “airplane”, I decided to finish what we started… :)

Finally!

I used a small plastic container for the front part, simply because I couldn’t find anything else that is transparent and could fit nicely.

Does the little guy love it?

Definitely!

Attention... This is your captain speaking...

He loves it so much that he even attempted to feed it with spaghetti bolognaise! >_<

It needs fuel to fly, Mommy!

I should have known that it wasn’t a great idea to let him play with it at lunch time… :P
