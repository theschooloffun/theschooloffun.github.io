Giggly Googly Octopus

Feb 23, 2011

There seems to be one animal character that the little guy consistently likes in most of the shows that he has watched so far: octopus.

Henry the Octopus in The Wiggles.

The octopus in Sesame Street. Like the one here.

Jup Jup in Hi-5. _uhm, not sure if that one really is an octopus… but it does look like one_

I say, we should have our own octopus … :)

What we had:

  1. paint

  2. paintbrush

  3. toilet paper roll

  4. wiggly eyes

  5. black marker

  6. glue

  7. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

We started off with the most exciting part: painting. The little guy seemed to be back in the mood for painting, so it was a breeze.

Something Purple

Then, we put on the wiggly eyes and drew the mouth. Yes, I finally had the confidence to use wiggly eyes. Let’s just say, I no longer have to worry that he would pop one of them into his mouth… :)

And finally, the final touch: cutting off the lower part of the toilet paper roll to make the tentacles.

Tadaa…

Our Very Own Giggly Googly Octopus

I could tell that the little guy kinda liked his new toy. I guess, it was partly due to the wiggly eyes. He loved shaking the octopus to see them go up and down and to hear the sound they made.

For an unknown reason, he also loved to “measure” it… :S

Busy Measuring

After his afternoon nap, he got busy playing with it again.

And guess what he brought to me after a few minutes?!

Uh oh...

No, he didn’t swallow the wiggly eyes. On his other hand, he kept the wiggly eyes. He gave them back to me and asked, “What ‘appen?”. Yeah.. as if he knew nothing about it! Geee….

That was it. No more googly-eyed octopus… >_<

At least the octopus had managed to entertain him for quite some time.

As for the wiggly eyes, they shalt not be wasted! We took one of the books we borrowed from the library and had a great time giggling… :)
