Hooray for Rainstick!

Feb 28, 2011

The other day, after we made our giggly googly octopus, we had some leftover paint. And surely, the easiest way not to waste is to paint another thing… :P Using just one color was rather boring, so I ended up adding more paint to the leftover … :P

The little guy had no problem taking over from there…

Easy Peasy!

I didn’t really have an idea of what to make then. It was only a few days later that I suddenly thought of a rainstick. At first, I wasn’t sure how to make it. Yet, thanks to Google, I got to learn how to make it… :)

I used toothpicks instead of nails and filled our tube with some soy beans and mung beans. For safety reason, I only made this when the little guy was fast asleep.

Not Yet Toddler-Proofed

Too bad the little guy woke up earlier than expected… He woke up when I was still testing how many beans I should put inside. I had to quickly seal the tube since he couldn’t wait to play with it. I should have put more beans … >_<

Nevertheless, the sound was great! All we have to do is just tilt a little, not too much, to make the sound last longer… :)

Raining...

You should try it, too… :)
