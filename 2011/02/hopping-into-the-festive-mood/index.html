Hopping into the Festive Mood

Feb 9, 2011

The festive Chinese New Year atmosphere is certainly still here.

Last weekend, we heard the thunderous beating of drums and cymbals every now and then. No doubt the sound must have come from the lion dance performances around the neighborhood.

Once, when we heard the music from the beating drums, my husband decided to put up a private show for the little guy… :)

Dancing to the rhythm

The little guy certainly enjoyed the show… :D For a while, I thought it would be a great idea to bring him to watch the real lion dance performance.

Then something changed my mind.

Or maybe, when we really bring him out to catch the real show, we should watch him closely… :P

One could just never guess what the little guy could do to the poor dancer.

I know it's you, Dad... Stop hiding!
