Living His Passion

Feb 17, 2011

I used to think that the little guy had a very short attention span. Much shorter than his peers, and too short for me to keep him occupied in one activity for more than 10 minutes.

Until the day he found his passion.

Cutting strawberries

He spent more than 15 minutes cutting strawberries. With full concentration. Into small pieces!

This wasn’t his first time using a knife. He had cut bananas many times since his first time. Yet, this one seemed to keep him occupied for the longest time. Ever.

It could probably be more than 30 minutes, if only we had more strawberries to cut.

More?

I still find it hard, though…

No, I don’t mind it’s hard to see that his attention span has grown.

It’s just hard to know that I would have to eat all that he has cut… :S You see, he doesn’t like to eat things that he has cut as much as he loves cutting them.

Uh oh…

Not another smashed strawberry! >_<
