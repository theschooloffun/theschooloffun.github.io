Paper on the Wall

Feb 22, 2011

One sunny afternoon and the little guy was somehow inspired to draw on one of the walls.

He took a pencil and climbed up on a chair, just to prop himself higher.

Mommy: No, no, we don’t draw on the walls. We draw on paper. Okie?

The little guy: _left his seat, sheepishly walking away to get a piece of paper_ Okie.

Mommy: _smiling_… _felt relieved for a moment_

The little guy: _returned with a piece of paper and started drawing on that piece of paper, after placing it on the same wall_

Paper on the Wall

Mommy: … _speechless_

There must be something about that wall that made him feel like he had to draw there. No matter what… >_<
