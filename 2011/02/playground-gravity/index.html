Playground Gravity

Feb 11, 2011

After a series of futile attempts involving sleeping (really) late at night and waking up (really) early in the morning to get more “me” time and to get things done, I realise I wasn’t the only one losing sleep… :S The little guy, too, seems to adjust his sleeping time, i.e. he sleeps later and wakes up earlier than usual. So, yeah… my long-awaited “me” time usually had to go down the drain the moment he was wide awake… :P

No surprise then if I was rather listless as we went for our usual morning round at the playground… :P Yet, somehow, this little guy didn’t seem to share the same lethargy! Gee… I wonder where he gets all the energy! That, of course, kinda frustrates him. Suddenly, unless there are other toddlers around, he loses his one and only partner for playing tag or hide and seek!

So, this morning, I devised a new mechanism.

It is called gravity.

No battery required. Hee hee… :P

All we need is just an empty bottle. And oh, yeah… a very energetic boy like the little guy never fails to make even the simplest game fun!

I just had to show him once that a bottle on a slide makes a great entertainment. He got the gist… straight away.

What mommy taught...

He could even improvise…

Improvisation #1: Slide from the Top

Improvisation #2: Slide and Catch

Improvisation #3: Race against the Bottle

All these while mommy was happily sitting down and taking pictures… _grinz_
