A Bittersweet Milestone

Mar 12, 2011

If you happen to have a kid of your own, do you often feel that sometimes you simply forget how much your kid has grown up?

I do.

The little guy is growing, at full speed… and I often think that he is still a small baby! As light as a small rice sack. And much shorter than our dining table.

And oh yeah, that latter assumption often gives me mess to clean up… :p

So, here’s a note to myself: Never ever leave a bottle of milk and a glass on the table for more than 5 minutes. The little guy can easily reach whatever that is on the table now (even if you think it is far from the edge!) and you never know what could happen!

Like the other day when the little guy rushed to find me in my room, saying, “wet… wet…”

The first thing that crossed my mind was … uhm, a wee wee accident. So, when I found him clean and dry with no sign of a wee wee accident, I felt relieved. But when he kept pulling me and saying, “wet…wet…”, I had no choice but to follow him to show me what actually happened.

And here is what he showed me.

My glass is overflowing...

Obviously, the little guy had poured the whole bottle of milk into his glass. Thankfully, there was just that much of milk left in the bottle to fill up the glass… ^_^” He didn’t bother to ask and wait for me to fill up his glass anymore! A good sign of independence… yet, it could also mean a major disaster if the bottle was full… :P

Anyway, the little guy then asked me to let him sit on the chair so that he could practice his drinking-without-holding-the-glass skill… :P

Experimenting on Different Ways to Drink

Of course, nobody got to cry over the spilled milk… ;) But somebody did have to clean up the mess… :P

Cleaning up the mess...

Good thing he helped me with the first stage of cleaning up… :)
