Blocks, etc.

Mar 28, 2011

I can’t remember precisely how we get to the point of having tons of blocks.

I do remember the first time we bought our first Mega Bloks, though. Nothing special. There are only 20 pieces. I guess we bought it simply because we were in love with the pull-along wagon.

Yet, two birthday parties later… and we now have lots of blocks, in different colors and different sizes.

Splendid!

We’ve been building lots of things: towers, dogs, giraffes, bridges… and some other unknown objects. Of course, most of the time, the little guy still follows the concept of “uBuild, mommy… and iDestroy”.

Lately, however, we have been using the blocks for other purposes… :)

Like, to hold the rulers in place as we bring Thomas for roller-coaster rides.

Buckle up, Thomas!

There is also something addictive about practicing using tong to grasp and dump some small blocks. Trust me, because of that, I haven’t been able to get that tong back to the kitchen. The little guy has declared the tong as his and he keeps it inside his block container!

Grasp and Dump

The little guy had also used that same plastic tong and different blocks to give a new twist to how to play the shape sorter toy.

Sorted with a Tong

And, speaking about sorting, those blocks also provide a great way to learn about colors.

Name That Color!

Hmm… I wonder what else we could use our blocks for… :D
