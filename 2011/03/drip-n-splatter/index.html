Drip 'n Splatter!

Mar 24, 2011

Okie. So, we’ve been trying different ways of painting.

But, there is still one method that I wish to explore, but I’m still mustering the courage to do it: splatter painting.

You know, just like Jackson Pollock’s.

I’m not that worried about the mess. We could always do it outside. I’m more worried that the little guy, a generous when it comes to pouring paint, would pour out the whole bottles of paint… >_<

Oh well, as we wait for that right moment, let’s just explore the digital version, shall we? ;)

It’s pretty addictive. The little guy often asks for it when he sees that the computer is on.

I managed to capture some of his artworks… :)
