Painting Spree!

Mar 8, 2011

Somebody’s been on a painting spree recently!

Once, he even asked to paint before bedtime… :S

… and before nap time… :S

When it first started, I was too tired to think of anything specific to paint. So, I just gave him a kitchen towel roll and let him paint with whichever color he chose.

The little guy, of course, chose all the colors! Even if that meant he had to wash the brush each time he was done with one … Or maybe, that was the part that made it all exciting… :P

Just keep painting... painting...

He still kept asking to paint that particular kitchen towel roll every now and then. But, until this mommy of his could think of what to make out of that roll, it would remain as an abstract roll for his painting spree… :P
