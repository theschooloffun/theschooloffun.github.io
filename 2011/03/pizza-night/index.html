Pizza Night!

Mar 21, 2011

In an attempt to entertain one grouchy toddler last week, we ransacked our freezer and got ourselves busy in the kitchen.

Uhm, okie, not really… The dining room, to be precise.

The loot from the freezer included frozen peas, mushrooms, ham chips, and shredded mozzarella. Just the perfect ingredients to make some pizza… :)

We didn’t have pizza dough. However, since we had plenty of bread at home, we could just skip that. We threw in some broccoli florets and pineapple… and we were all set to make our own pizza… ;)

The ingredients for a (hopefully) grouchy-free afternoon

First, I taught the little guy how to flatten the bread. He didn’t have much problem with this, since we had used a rolling pin when we played playdoh before. In fact, he enjoyed it so much that he wanted to flatten some peas, too! :S

The Rolling Monster

Next, he helped me spread some tomato paste on the bread before finally placing the toppings.

Putting some toppings...

Of course, not all toppings went to the pizza… :P

...and popping some into his mouth

The fun continued as we waited and watched the mozzarella melt.

Melting Fun

Pizza for dinner, you ask?

Nope, the little guy didn’t have pizza for dinner that night. Being a truly Asian little guy, he still prefers rice… >_<

There was still something to be grateful for, though. This was the first time the little guy prepared dinner for me… :) _grinz_
