Ever since I gained that confidence that the little guy wouldn’t pop any beads into his mouth, wow… the possibilities are simply endless!

We could thread beads onto a wire.

Or, we could just open the Playdoh’s lid, poke some spaghetti and start threading.

Bead Sticks
