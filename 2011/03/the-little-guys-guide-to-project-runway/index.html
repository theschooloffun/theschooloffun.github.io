The Little Guy's Guide to Project Runway

Mar 11, 2011

The little guy still loves it when I paint his hands and feet. So, one afternoon, when he was fast asleep, I got the ‘runway’ ready… ;)

Ready to Paint

You could see that I had foreseen the mess. I prepared a bowl of water on one side and a pack of wet tissue on the other side. Somehow, the picture of an active toddler with hands and feet full of paint got me a bit worried… :P

After the little guy woke up from his beauty nap, we got ourselves busy painting and jumping around at the same time. Yes, I really mean “we”! I decided to paint my toes and joined the fun… :)

Dancing in Colors!

I only left him on his own when he started crawling around. While he was busy on his own, I went to prepare his “pot of gold”: a bowl of green bean soup… :)

The moment I put the bowl down was exactly the moment the little guy left the runway to snatch his pot of gold. It was quite a struggle to persuade him to wash his hands and feet first!

A Pot of Gold at the End of the Rainbow

He continued with his project runway after he finished his snack.

We then left our project runway to dry overnight and took a picture of it the following morning.

Project Runway: Completed!

No walls were harmed in the making… :)
