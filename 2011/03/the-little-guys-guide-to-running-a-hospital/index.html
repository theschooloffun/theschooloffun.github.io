The Little Guy's Guide to Running a Hospital

Mar 25, 2011

Certainly, spending time with a busy toddler is never an easy task for those who are sensitive to noise.

No, I’m not one of them. I must admit, though, when he first started pushing the towel rack around the house, I winced at the sound it made. I nearly shouted at him, pleading him to stop that irritating sound.

Yet, after seeing the little guy pushing my towel rack around with his beloved dinosaur on top, I decided to run a hospital with him instead… :)

One Patient from the A & E!

Our story began with Dino coming in from the Accident & Emergency department. We tried to tickle him, but he didn’t respond… so, we declared that he had suffered a loss of consciousness…:P

Done with the Treatment

The little guy, M.D. then decided to wrap a bandage around the head and place adhesive bandages near his nose. Uh oh… I hope they didn’t block Dino’s nose… >_<

Don’t look at me, it was the doctor’s idea. I was too busy with the camera to show him what to do… :P Besides, I didn’t want to spoil the fun… :)

I must claim, however, that our hospital delivers first-class services. Just see what the little guy did next.

A Special Kiss-it-better

He kissed Dino and wished him to get well soon! Awww… so sweet of him!

Anyway, it was quite a busy day at the hospital. So, the little guy couldn’t really spend much time with Dino. He then dashed to see his other patient, Duckie.

A Jab for Duckie

I wasn’t sure what happened to Duckie, but the little guy decided to just give him a jab and moved him to another ward (i.e. on the table… no more comfy bed for you, Duckie!).

Or, perhaps, the little guy just thought that Duckie’s case wasn’t that exciting. Plus, we needed a place for our next patient: Whalee.

Coming in...!

I wondered what happened to Whalee… It was just getting more exciting!

In the end, I couldn’t resist to stop taking pictures and just join the fun… >:P We handled quite a number of patients that day!

And hey, do you know something, the sound didn’t bother me anymore! ;)
