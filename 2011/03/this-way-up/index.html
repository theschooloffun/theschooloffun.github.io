This Way Up

Mar 24, 2011

I wasn’t just taking picture of a table.

(Once) A Coffee Table

Go on. Take a closer look.

Yeah.. that’s the little guy under the table, with his palette full of paint.

We are still in the midst of exploring different ways of painting. And a couple of days ago was certainly the right time to introduce the little guy to a different perspective of painting… :)

This time round, we placed our drawing paper underneath the coffee table. I also put some cushions under the table, so that the little guy would still feel comfortable while painting.

A Different Perspective

I found this kind of painting challenging, but fun. Obviously, the little guy found it exciting, too. We kept the drawing paper there for a few days as he kept asking to explore different methods and “enhance” his painting… :P

Or, in his own words, “color… more…”

Exploring One Method...

..and Another

Hm… forget about the discomfort from tilting the head while painting. I wonder if Michelangelo shared the same excitement when he painted Sistine Chapel ceiling. Hee hee… :P

Anyway, this was how our Sistine Chapel ceiling looks like.
