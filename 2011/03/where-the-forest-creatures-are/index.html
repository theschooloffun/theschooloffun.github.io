Where the Forest Creatures Are

Mar 25, 2011

Remember this forest of ours?

Well, we decided to make full use of it… :)

Sticking the Forest Creatures

All we need is just a clear plastic folder and some stickers… :) Just place the painting inside the folder, and we’ve got ourselves a perfect thing to keep the little guy occupied for a few minutes while we are at the church!

I’m so glad I didn’t throw the sticker base straight away. The little guy somehow thinks it is fun to place the stickers back after putting all the stickers on the forest.

Placing the Stickers Back

Or maybe he simply loves matching the stickers to the silhouettes on the base. Just like solving a jigsaw puzzle… :)

Not bad for someone who didn’t seem to be interested in jigsaw puzzles a few months ago…. :P
