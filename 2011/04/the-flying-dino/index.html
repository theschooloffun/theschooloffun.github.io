The Flying Dino

Apr 15, 2011

I’ve finally figured out what to do with one of the little guy’s masterpieces… :)

So, before it got accidentally thrown, I quickly went to find the picture of dinosaur that my husband drew for us months ago. Using that picture as a pattern, we soon had two dinosaurs ready to be cut… :)

Ready to Cut

I did think of making more dinosaurs. Too bad the little guy took over the black marker and drew on the paper while I was busy cutting… >_< Unless we don’t mind to have black dinosaurs, I guess we just had to accept this kind of cause for the extinction of the dinosaurs… :P Two dinosaurs are good enough.

After I finished cutting the dinosaurs, the little guy decided to run a puppet show.

The Talking Dinos

He seemed to enjoy his own show so much that I didn’t have the heart to continue with my plan. Obviously, he didn’t know that his two dinosaurs would soon be glued into one… >:P

I waited until he left the two dinosaurs alone before I glued the two dinosaurs on the cardboard that I had prepared.

And, finally… :)

The One and Only of Its Kind

There are actually two holes on the middle part of the cardboard. Those are meant for us to put our fingers in.

Here, let the little guy show you…

Ready for the Walking Dino?

We can then just wiggle our fingers inside, rocking the dinosaurs back and forth, so that it would look as if the dinosaur is walking.

But, of course, the little guy thought walking was not fun for the dinosaur at all.

Flying was much more fun!

Here Comes Super-Dino!
