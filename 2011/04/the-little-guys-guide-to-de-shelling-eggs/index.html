The Little Guy's Guide to De-shelling Eggs

Apr 20, 2011

A couple of days ago, our dinner menu included egg mayo salad.

The little guy kindly offered his help to de-shell the hard-boiled eggs… :P

The First Step

Although I taught him to first tap the eggs on a hard surface, he still thought that his way was much more fun: breaking each egg into two. Surprisingly, each time he somehow managed to keep the yolk as round as a ball.

Next, since the eggs were already broken into two, I taught him how to “peel” the shells.

The Second Step

I was surprised he actually persevered! Hee hee… I thought he would stop after the first half.

Anyway, here is what we got:

Well done!

Thankfully, the eggs were meant for egg mayo salad. The eggs were to be mashed anyway… :P

While I got busy mashing the eggs, the little guy occupied himself “mashing” the shells.

Smashing Shells

Although he proved himself well, especially as a first-timer, this little kitchen assistant obviously did not come cheap. He charged me a big glass of grass jelly drink after the salad was ready… :P
