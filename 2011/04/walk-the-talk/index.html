Walk the Talk

Apr 6, 2011

One sunny afternoon and the little guy was playing with two horses and a car.

When he started banging one of the horses and shouted, “Please… please”, never would I have suspected that he was teaching the horses a lesson.

Whose Turn?

A lesson on sharing, it was … :)

The little guy was actually running a kind of show. There was only 1 car, so the two horses had to share the car. The horse that shouted “Please… please” was simply asking for its turn to ride the car. It also politely said “Thank you” after it finally got its turn.

Cute!

Let’s just hope that this little preacher continues to practice what he preaches about sharing… :P
