A Cake Story

May 17, 2011

This was what happened when the little guy refused to take a nap when his mommy was dying to try out a recipe: the little guy got to bake a butter cake! :)

It was kinda difficult at first. I would say it took me longer to complete the whole thing.

More mess to clean up was inevitable. No one else was around at home. Otherwise, I could have had an extra pair of eyes to watch what the little guy was doing and prevent any spill.

The little guy also enjoyed the whole experience. So, for certain things, I decided to just let him play for a little longer.

But, overall, it wasn’t a recipe for disaster to get him involved… ;) We both had fun!

So, what exactly did he get to do?

  1. Measurement

This part was the reason why I said “it was kinda difficult at first”. I had to do this part, yet at the same time, I had to make sure the little guy didn’t add anything to what I’d measured.  And yup, I also had to make sure he didn’t throw or spill anything… :P

He was already very excited by then. So, to keep him away from my butter and flour, I let him measure the raisins. Obviously not the main ingredients… so I really didn’t mind if he didn’t put enough… :P And even if he put more than enough, I certainly wouldn’t object either. The more, the merrier….;) _grinz_

Yummy Raisins!

He also helped me mixing the raisins with some flour… :)

A Serious Mixing

2. Preparing the Tin

After I greased the tin, I let him do the flouring part.

Flour Splattering in Progress...

He must have thought the tin was a kind of drum… :P He kept tapping the tin and giggled!

3. Sifting the Flour

I know, I know… Everything about it spells messy.

Writing on the Flour

Although it was easy to see that he enjoyed playing with the flour very much, it wasn’t that great an idea to actually let him do this part. Hey, I did try to minimize the mess… I laid down newspaper underneath. So, everything seemed to be under control at first… :P

Then I went to put the flour … lo and behold, somebody tried to be helpful! The little guy followed behind me, pulling the newspaper with him… :P

Yup, we’ve got flour …all over the floor!  >_<

4. Mixing

Ah, the joy when we finally reached this part!

Are We There Yet?

The little guy loved it when I held his hand along with the mixer. He also loved to play with the spatula… thankfully, he didn’t think of flicking a spatula full of the cake batter… :P

5. Cleaning Up

Yayy.. almost done!

And the Dish Ran Away with the Spoon...

He actually helped me with the dishes! :’)

And, finally… our big reward… tadaaa… ^o^

Yumm-O!
