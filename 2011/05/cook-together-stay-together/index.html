Cook Together, Stay Together

May 22, 2011

I once saw a tagline that goes “A family that cooks together stays together”. It may just be another version of “A family that prays together stays together”. Still, I think it was worth trying.

Cookie Cutting

And true enough, at least for that very few minutes, all three of us stayed around the dining table as we baked our own cookies… :) _grinz_

The Part where He Ran Away

It could be longer, if only we didn’t finish the cookies in such a short time… :P

Happily Munching His "Boats"

While the little guy might still be too young to have that sense of accomplishment, I must admit that I’ve learned a lot after these cooking together episodes. I learn to stop being too much of a mommy that interferes as the little guy tries to figure out things himself, with his own ways. Yup, interference is futile… :P And that also means that I get to learn to let go and not to clean compulsively all the time.

My goodness… I think I even learn to enjoy the mess!!!
