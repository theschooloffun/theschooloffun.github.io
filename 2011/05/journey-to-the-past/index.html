Journey to the Past

May 9, 2011

If you ever run out of ideas of what to do with your restless toddler, you might want to consider bringing them to a nursing home.

Yes, I wasn’t joking. A nursing home, indeed!

You’ll be surprised by how well your toddlers and the residents there could entertain each other. At least, I was pleasantly surprised during our last visit… :)

Playing Connect Four

Perhaps it was because it wasn’t our first visit and the little guy has finally felt at ease with the people there. Or perhaps that Connect Four game helped them…

Whichever the reason is, I’m just glad they both enjoyed themselves… :)

And oh, by the way, here’s a little treat for the little guy at the end of the day: he got to push the wheelchair around… ;)

Fun Ride

Oh yeah, he loves it!

Thankfully, he was very careful, too. So, I guess, the lady was just as happy as the little guy was… ;)

Wait a minute… that wasn’t all. There was a little bonus, too!

The little guy got the chance to admire the fish tank. With up close view!

Here, Fishy!

What a trip! :)
