Our Tray Overfloweth!

May 17, 2011

Something that used to be cute now begins to irk me.

Remember how the little guy loves to hide his dinosaurs inside the rice box? Well, he also likes to hide his alphabets, only to dig and search for them later on, flicking rice grains all over the floor… >_<

You see, I don’t mind if he plays with the rice grains. But throwing rice grains is a BIG NO-NO. In fact, I find it rude to waste food.

Lecturing the little guy doesn’t seem to show any result so far. So, to appease both of us, I lend him a small ice cube tray and a plastic spoon.

Each a Spoonful


... and Ready for Replay

There you go… as long as he keeps his hands inside the box, I could expect no more wasted grain.

Let’s just hope he won’t get bored too soon… ^_^”
