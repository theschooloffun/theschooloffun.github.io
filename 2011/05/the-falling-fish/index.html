The Falling Fish

May 9, 2011

I’d been wanting to try making this blow fish toy ever since I saw it.

The Little Propeller that Could

Yet, when we finally managed to make our own fish, somehow neither of us could make the fish move by blowing… :P We could only move the fish by pushing and pulling it along the line. Probably the combination of the wrong straw (we used a big straw) and the wrong kind of string (we used a thick yarn) just makes our fish sluggish.

Still, the little guy loves it. So, I decided to put one end higher. That way, we could just leave it to the gravity to make the fish zoom down the line… ;)

Dive in, Fish!

It was also just a matter of time to see another favorite thing of his being added to the line.

Choo...Choo...

Very simple to make, yet very fun to play with… :)

Sadly, while no fish was harmed during this game, I must admit that my wall became a victim as soon as we started playing… :(

Our Poor Wall

Obviously, the little guy thought he was so light that the yarn could sustain his weight as he jumped around… :P He must have thought that yarn was a kind of jumping rope… >_<

I should be more careful next time… ^_^”
