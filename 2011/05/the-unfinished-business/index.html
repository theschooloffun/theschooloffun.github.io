The Unfinished Business

May 30, 2011

I haven’t quite finished sewing this dog, which I made from an old pair of socks.

The Unfinished Business

There are still a gap near its tail to seal and four feet to sew.

I stopped sewing after seeing that it didn’t look as cute as it should be. It didn’t even look like a dog… >_<

But somebody has recently lifted up my spirit… :)

Best Friends

The little guy has been playing with it pretty often after he snatched it from my sewing table that day. And each time he holds it, he would make that “woof… woof” sound and tell me, “mommy… look! Look! Doggy!”.

I haven’t even told him that it was supposed to be a dog! So, I guess it does look like a dog, somehow!

Gee, maybe I really should get it done quickly… :)
