Jun 29, 2011

After a fulfilling 2-week holiday in Indonesia, we’re finally back in Singapore.

And while mommy was busy with the damage control _looking proudly at the tall pile of clothes to wash and iron that is slowly decreasing…_, the little guy just had to entertain himself.

With anything possible…

Cleaning His Sandals

Even my old sequins were given a chance…

Construction Sites with Sequins

Surprisingly, of all the things in the house, he seemed to miss his humble toy collection the most, such as the fish and the truck and the fishing game. He didn’t even ask me to turn on the PC like he usually did!

Weird! Really!

But I guess that speaks volumes about what a toddler remembers and hopefully what he would continue to cherish. He never cares about whether a toy comes with an expensive price tag. Or whether it is featured on TV or the talk of the town.

Often, the joy is indeed in the simple things.

And I’m glad both my husband and I could be part of those childhood memories that the little guy could treasure as he grows up… :)
