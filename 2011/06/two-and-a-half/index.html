Two and a Half

Jun 9, 2011

We recently celebrated the little guy’s 2 1/2 birthday… :)

The special treats for the day? Well, first, he could finally bring his bike, which we bought for him last month, out from the storeroom.

After practising delayed gratification for more than a week, he could finally unwrap his precious gift. What a joy!

Finally!

And guess whom he brought along for a ride?

The Lucky Passenger

I think I need to find or make him a proper basket for his bike soon… :P

That first treat, however, wasn’t quite his most favorite. The second treat was.

The second treat involves getting him to sing the happy birthday song and blow out a candle.

Again!

He really loved that second treat! He made sure the candle was lit again as soon as he blew it out.

I think we must have done this whole thing a thousand times on that special day!
