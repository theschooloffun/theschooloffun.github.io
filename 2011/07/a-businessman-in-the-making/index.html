A Businessman in the Making?!

Jul 29, 2011

I don’t know whether it’s a good thing or bad.

Whenever he asks me for some coins to buy a small bottle of milk (which costs slightly above SGD 1) or a 1-dollar coin to unlock a coin-lock supermarket trolley, he would ask me for, “Five dollars!”

_gasp_ It really makes it sound as if he wants to log hefty profit every time we make a trip to the supermarket!

I know, it is probably easier just to think positive and claim that he’s a businessman in the making… >:P But, I guess it really is just because he recently likes number five a lot… :P

He certainly has some learning to do!
