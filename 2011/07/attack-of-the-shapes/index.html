Attack of the Shapes

Jul 26, 2011

This is what happened to our living room as we learned about shapes last week.

Shapes, Everywhere!

The rule of the game was that the little guy had to close his eyes and take one of the shapes I made using construction paper.

Pick-A-Shape

He then had to run or jump into the same shape while shouting the name of the shape.

But, of course, mommy’s rules are meant to be broken… :P As you could see in the picture above, he preferred to use the masking tape as roads for his cars.

That evening, with his daddy, he even drew on one of the shapes to turn it into a railway!

Quick, Daddy... Thomas needs this railway!

Meanwhile, that night, as I lied on the bed, I kept thinking how on earth I would be able to sweep and mop the floor the following morning. I was pretty sure that the little guy wouldn’t let me take off any of those shapes.

And, yup, I was right. The following morning and many mornings after, he shouted at me whenever he saw me trying to peel the tape. He watched closely as I cleaned the house, making sure I wouldn’t take any of his shapes away.

He also created different kinds of games for himself to play with his shapes, like this matching game.

The Matching Game

And finally, after putting up with these masking tapes for nearly a week, I managed to peel them off first thing in the morning before he woke up. I made sure I left no trace behind. In fact, it was so clean that even the little guy didn’t remember anything about it! Great! :)
