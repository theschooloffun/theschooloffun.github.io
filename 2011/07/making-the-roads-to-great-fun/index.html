Making the Roads to Great Fun!

Jul 12, 2011

I’ve harbored the idea of making a kind of play mat for the little guy for months. And from month to month, the little guy just had to bear with the small map that I drew for him every now and then.

The A3-Size Play Mat

No wonder traffic jams are always inevitable… :P

Then one day, I finally sat down and stole my husband’s trusted sharpie. It didn’t take me that long to draw a map on a piece of paper… Yeah, I also wonder why I didn’t do this long, long time ago! Gee…

Easy Peasy!

And since then, this simple play mat has worked wonder in keeping the little guy occupied!

Still a Traffic Jam...

He made all kinds of stories in his own words each time he lined his cars, trucks or trains there.

Sometimes, he would bring his farm animals to the main road, too… :P Especially when he’s learning his alphabets. _grinz_

At the Crossroads

It’s amazing that there hasn’t been any casualty yet… :P
