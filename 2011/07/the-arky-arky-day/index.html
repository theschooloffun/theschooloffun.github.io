The Arky Arky Day

Jul 14, 2011

So we are both (finally) down with cough… Yet, how the little guy could still be up and about in no time is still a mystery. He seems to have lots of energy while his mommy, running a mild fever after catching the flu bug from him, could only sleep like a log the whole afternoon… >_<

Of course, somebody still had to entertain the little guy while the medicine knocked me out…

And so came the story of Noah’s Ark to save the day… :)

Reading the Script

You see, just give a toddler a baby bath tub and a set of animal figurines and you’ve got yourself the basic props for the reenactment!

For half an hour or so, he totally forgot that I was there, sleeping on the couch… :P

...arky... arky... twosies... twosies...

It really helps to have the right songs to sing along, too… :) _although I couldn’t really sing…sob sob…_

Never mind if it was hard to assure him that Noah wouldn’t need that train set…

Everything was fine and dandy dandy...

He could put whatever he likes… as long as I could have that beauty sleep… :)
