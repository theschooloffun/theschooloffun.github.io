The Breathing Space

Jul 18, 2011

Call it a blessing in disguise.

Getting Busy with his A-to-Z beads

After the two episodes of illnesses (i.e. stomach flu and cough) that prevented me from keeping the little guy company as much as usual, I realize his attention span seems to have improved. While I can’t say for sure whether it really is due to the back-to-back episodes or it is just part of growing up, I must say I’m glad he could now play on his own longer.

Yup, that certainly gives me some breathing space! So, if you happen to long for this breathing space, too… don’t worry, there is still hope that your turn may be next! ;)

Let’s just pray this is not going to be a mere stint… :) As for now, I shall just enjoy it while it lasts…
