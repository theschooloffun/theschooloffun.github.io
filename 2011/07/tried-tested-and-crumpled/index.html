Tried, Tested and Crumpled

Jul 18, 2011

While browsing some books in Daiso, I stumbled across how to make a very simple paper airplane. I quickly made a mental note to make such airplane with the little guy.

I couldn’t really remember the exact design, but it should look roughly something like this.

The Template

All we need to do next is to fold in the middle and cut to make the full airplane from the “half” template. Make more folds to make the airplane stand and voila…

Ready to Fly

The little guy was certainly happy with his new toy and appointed himself as the pilot for the day. He brought it around and played with it along with his cars and trucks.

Performing Emergency Landing

Then he figured out that he could actually turn the airplane into a crumple …

Crumpled and Packed

And that’s the end of the airplane… _ouch!_
