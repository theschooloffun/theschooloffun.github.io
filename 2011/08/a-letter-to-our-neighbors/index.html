A Letter to Our Neighbors

Aug 1, 2011

Dear neighbors,

We’re sorry if you’ve been hearing a weird, repetitive drumming sound from our house. It isn’t that we’ve just bought ourselves a new drum kit and couldn’t stop to play a beat. It is just that our little guy has just re-purposed our drying rack and his markers as his drum kit.

Got Rhythm?

Please don’t think we have something against you either if you start hearing somebody singing out of tune. I’m afraid it’s our little guy’s vocal style once he gets too excited during his drumming session. And, boy, how he gets excited very easily!

Thank you for your kind understanding.

Regards,

Your friendly neighbor

P.S: Don’t worry. We don’t intend to buy him a real drum kit anytime soon…
