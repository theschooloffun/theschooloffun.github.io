Another Caterpillar!

Aug 12, 2011

Two weeks ago, for our usual weekly playdate, I thought I should prepare something for the kids to play with.

Something Green

We planned to go to a local park. Park. Green. I just couldn’t help but to think for something around green and of course, cute.

And so along came our little caterpillar….

Honk!

Easy to make and easy to bring along. All you need to do is wrap a pipe cleaner (or chenille stem) around a pencil a couple of time, creating a kind of coil. After you take the coil out from the pencil, make sure you hide all the sharp ends. You certainly wouldn’t want this cute little caterpillar to hurt anybody… :) Just add wiggly eyes and small antennas made from construction paper and you have a new friend to play with.

No special diet needed for this cute little pet, but you certainly should bring it out often… ;)
