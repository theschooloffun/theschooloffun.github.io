Beware: Angry Birds Ahead!

Aug 1, 2011

Somebody got to wake up to a special bird this morning… :)

Ready to Snooze

After running around all week long with insufficient “me” time, I finally declared last night as a “me” night and left the little guy in the care of my husband.

I know. Isn’t he sweeeet?! :) And nope, I’m not talking about the little guy… >:P

Anyway, in less than an hour, I managed to complete three angry birds for the little guy to play with and for my hubby to admire … _yup, he just has to coo over them!_ >:P

The first one is made of felt. It is soft and fluffy. Thankfully! ‘Cos the little guy obviously loves firing his angry bird around! I bet the next in his wish list would be a catapult…. >_<

Bull's Eye!

Of course, there’s got to be something rather educational to make me feel better about this whole angry bird making thingy, right?! _grinz_

So, tadaaa… introducing: angry bird and happy bird! After all, what could be the best way to teach a child about different kinds of emotions if not by using his favorite characters?! _nod, nod_ Mwahaha… isn’t that just killing two pigs with one bird, huh?!

Angry vs. Happy

Yeah, right… :P

Oh well… At least they have all joined the ranks of the little guy’s storytelling characters… :) And this mother of his just has to admit that she loves listening to his stories, even though she still finds it hard to understand the gibberish part… :P
