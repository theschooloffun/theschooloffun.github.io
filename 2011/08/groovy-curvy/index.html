Groovy Curvy

Aug 4, 2011

Somebody’s into maze nowadays…

Curvy

And I can’t believe I have been that patient, drawing him one maze after another… :S! Taking care of this toddler seems to make me have more patience!

It took me a total of 2 pieces of A3-size paper (we used both sides!) and many pieces (I’ve lost count!) of A4-size paper (also both sides!) before it finally dawned on me that the little guy actually didn’t mind reusing the same maze.

Duh!

Groovy

Only then did I start drawing the maze on A4-size paper. Then, I could just insert it into a clear sheet protector and let the little guy scribble away with a marker. When he’s done, we could just erase the marker using a tissue and we are ready to start over again!

Groovy Curvy!

Bwahaha… It turns out that taking care of the little guy doesn’t actually make me more patient. It does make me more resourceful, though… :P
