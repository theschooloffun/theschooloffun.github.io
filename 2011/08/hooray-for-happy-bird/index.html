Hooray for Happy Bird!

Aug 22, 2011

Don’t you just love seeing the potential?

The Potential

The recent trip to IKEA has indeed made me a couple bucks poorer (Total Damage: 1 red hand towel + 1 red wash cloth) but I’ve been grinning from ear to ear each time I open the wardrobe and see this cute hooded towel.

Happy Bird in the Making

The actual damage also includes some old and new (a free gift!) towels. I’m pretty sure my husband is still planning of a way to get another free towel to replace that yellow towel which I have happily cut for my bird… :P

Although he kept saying “Nice, Mommy!” when I was busy sewing the hooded towel, the little guy refused to cooperate when I asked him to model the new hooded towel… >_<

I guess for a while I simply forgot that since young, he doesn’t like to have anything covering his head. Not a cap. Not even a hood.

Nope, not even an ice cream could change his mind…

No!

But, don’t worry… Frosty came to the rescue… :)

Frosty and the Hood

Awww… now I feel like making another one…
