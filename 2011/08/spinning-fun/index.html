Spinning Fun!

Aug 29, 2011

When I first read about Salad Spinner Painting in MaryAnn F. Kohl’s Preschool Art: It’s the Process, Not the Product, I almost went all the way buying a salad spinner. How could I resist, the activity seems just too cool to miss… :(

Not a big fan of hoarding big, single-task kitchen tools, I finally managed to put aside that idea. Instead, I decided to repurpose one of our pan lids for our very own kind of spinner painting.

Keep Spinning!

Doesn’t it just look like a lot fun, too?! ;)

Well, it certainly was fun, although I should have thinned the paint a little bit more. We had to keep giving the lid a good, hard spin before we could utilize the centrifugal force to create any nice design… :S

In the end, the little guy just couldn’t take it anymore. He started playing with the paint. And guess what… our spin painting activity soon turned into a finger painting session… :P
