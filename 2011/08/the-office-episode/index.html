The Office Episode

Aug 22, 2011

After getting more “No!”s than I could take from the little guy each time I suggested an activity, I finally gave in and let him decide what he wanted to do instead.

I’m not sure if he really meant it or if it was because of his recent preference for his daddy over me, but he blurted “Daddy… Office.”

And so, we drew out our plan… _grinz_ or rather, it was a map to guide us on how to go to daddy’s office and what daddy usually does in the office… :P

The Map

Wearing one of his fave shirts, the little guy then “cycled” to daddy’s office.

While he was cycling to the office, our living room miraculously turned into an office with a nice typewriter and some hardworking bears (and a duck!) in crisp-looking shirts.

In the Office

Each of them was busy working on something. Oops.. that duck obviously read the wrong book… :P

The little guy quickly chose a role for himself, working intensely on the typewriter. Every now and then he would summon one of the bears and get it to work on the typewriter, too…

Your Turn, PeeWee...

For five days, this storyline seemed to continue be his favorite. He would ask me for the typewriter first thing in the morning. I even start to think that maybe it really was a good idea to get him to choose for himself…  :)

Too bad this happened on the fifth day…

The Long, Messy Ribbon

Obviously, I didn’t realize that he had changed to another role on the fifth day! He probably had turned himself into a mechanic or simply someone who wanted to do a reverse engineering… :S

Poor typewriter! :(
