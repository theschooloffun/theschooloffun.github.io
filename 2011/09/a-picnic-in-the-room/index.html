A Picnic in the Room

Sep 26, 2011

Really…

Sometimes rules are meant to be broken.

I never thought I would dare to even have the thought of having a picnic… in the bedroom… But look, we’ve recently had a picnic in the little guy’s bedroom… :p

Enjoying the Company of Dino

The fact that the lunch menu was rather of no-mess kind also gave me the courage to let the little guy bring in his glass of chocolate milk and invite his good friend, Dino.

And that day I learned that the little guy could actually eat properly, leaving no mess behind. Geee… if only we could do this every day… I bet he could then have more practice to eat properly… :P
