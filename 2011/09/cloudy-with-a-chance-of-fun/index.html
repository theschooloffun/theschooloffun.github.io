Cloudy with a Chance of Fun

Sep 9, 2011

Bet you don’t know this little secret!

Private Beach

The best time to visit the beach is on a cloudy day. It has to be cloudy enough so that everyone else would be discouraged to go to the beach. Yet, certainly, you have to pray hard that it wouldn’t rain when you’re there… :P

We’ve got that perfect combo last weekend and it felt great to have the beach almost to ourselves. It wasn’t as crowded as usual, especially during this school holiday period! Amazing!

We even got the chance to take the luge rides without getting stuck in a long queue!

Once is Certainly Never Enough!

We’d certainly love to do this again another time… ;)
