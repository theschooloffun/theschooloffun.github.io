Sew... A Needle Pulling Thread...!

Sep 19, 2011

Another good thing about the frequent long trips to the playground is that the little guy now finds there’s nothing novel when I sew something. At least not when I sew by hand. The sewing machine still has to wait… :)

He used to be so curious that he would quickly climb to my lap and the next thing I know the fabric would be in his hands. Or the needle… >_<

But now, well… he would simply continue with whatever he is doing. If the sewing activity approaches the maximum period of “mommy’s inactivity” he could endure, he would just ask me “What’s that, mommy?” a million times more than the number of  times I could patiently reply him … :P Only as soon as the period of mommy’s inactivity goes beyond what he could take would he start to climb to my lap or pull my hands while saying, “Mommy, don’t want sewing…”

Not bad, indeed…

On certain wonderful days, he would even entertain himself with the scraps from my fabric stash.
