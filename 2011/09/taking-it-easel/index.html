Taking It Easel

Sep 28, 2011

The recent storeroom spring cleaning brought us an old easel that had long been buried under all kinds of other stuff. The moment I brought it out to the living room, the little guy claimed that it is “nice!” and hence, it was his to play with all day long…

It certainly makes a great easel (well, of course!), although we haven’t really done any painting with it.

With a Pencil

The little guy attempted to work out a masterpiece, but I think, in the end, he was more fascinated by the fact that the base could hold a lot of colored pencils… :P

With Colored Pencils

He also figured out that the tower could make a great hiding place for his pencil…Geee, and I actually spent so much time trying to find that pencil! He must be laughing at me when he giggled then… :P

Spot the Pencil

His favorite game of all? He somehow figured out that when appropriately loosened, the delimiter made a great oscillating woodpecker kind of toy! All he needs to do is to bring it all the way and it’ll keep on bobbing all the way down…

Keep On Bobbing and Bobbing...

Fun, indeed!
