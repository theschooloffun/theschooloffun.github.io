The Playground Preschool

Sep 13, 2011

Since last month, our usual morning trip to the playground just seems to get longer and longer. It even stretched to two hours during last week’s school holiday… >_<

And it was all started with a very simple thing: lacing.

You see, it took me forever to get the little guy into lacing. Even using his favorite characters doesn’t seem to work.

Abandoned...

Yet, somehow, this 7-year-old girl whom the little guy often meets at the playground could make the little guy interested in lacing within minutes!!! Nope, she doesn’t use a cord. Instead, she uses tree leaves that are scattered around the playground.

Hooray for Lacing!

Since then, the two of them become good buddies. They could spend hours at the playground, lacing or simply monkeying around on the monkey bars… ^_^”

Geeee…

Of course, I just have to entertain myself while the two of them play together. So, tadaaa…
