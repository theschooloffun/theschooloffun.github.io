Oh, Naptime!

Oct 21, 2011

And so the little guy and I recently made a little pact…:)

He could skip naps if he plays quietly… on his own. A great pact, indeed. It spares me from any unnecessary struggle to get him to sleep, while I get things done around the house or simply have a brief “me” time.

Most of the time, he does fall asleep after he’s done playing on his own.

Like yesterday. He went to sleep after completing his “secret” project.

His Favorite: Carwash

Obviously, while I wasn’t paying attention, he helped himself with the bucket and the water tap. He then dipped each car inside his car box into the water, giving it a good wash, I suppose, and formed a line of cars next to the bucket.

Messy? Well, a bit. Amusing? Definitely!

And surely, we both had a great afternoon… :) He slept like a log after completing that project, and I didn’t even have to waste my breath trying to win any naptime battle… _grinz_
