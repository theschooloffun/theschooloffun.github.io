Remember the Paint

Oct 20, 2011

For a while, I almost forgot how the little guy’s brushstroke looks like…

Wet Look

For a while, I almost forgot how it feels when our living room turns into a studio…

His Little Studio

I nearly forgot how generous the little guy could be with the paint… >_<

Dollops of Paint!

… and that life with this guy who never likes wearing an apron could never be stain-free!

Loving the Washable!

I nearly forgot how, wielding his brush, he could turn himself into a samurai… painting the sofa cover and floor, though not deliberately. I am just glad he missed my lens!

Watch out, Mommy!

I almost forgot how much he loves to paint himself, too… Exactly, I almost forgot how a knee full of paint looks like…

A Knee Full of Paint

… and how much he loves painting his cars and buses, too…

Colorful Wheels

Most importantly, I should have remembered that I must clean him and distract him with something else to play before I clean up the rest of the mess. Well, unless I have the uncanny ability to predict what brilliant ideas he may have…

Carwash

Like washing his cars with the water that we earlier used to wash the brushes…

Second Round of Carwash

.. or stepping on the wet palette… >_<”

Gee…

I almost forgot all those important notes, until the little guy gave me a crash course yesterday morning. Obviously, it just warmed my heart that he asked to paint first thing in the morning when he usually asks to go to the playground instead. It probably also sent my body into a shock when we ended up painting for nearly an hour!

Oh yeah… now it’s certainly not going to be easy for me to forget that behind each simple painting, there’s always a complex, if not interesting, story to tell… :P

Just the way I think life with kids should be… :)
