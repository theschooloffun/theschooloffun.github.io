Splotches, Blotches and Bubbles

Oct 6, 2011

A while ago, we came across Little Blue and Little Yellow by Leo Lionni in the library. This interesting book tells about the adventure of Little Blue and Little Yellow in simple words. But here is what I like most: the splotches of color… :)

Here Comes the Green!

I’m pretty sure the little guy shared the same excitement as we began our own little project related to this book. The whole experiment may not be something new to him, but the pipette surely was!

It didn’t take him long to finally master the art of using a pipette: Squeeze - Dip - Release to fill the pipette up and finally Squeeeeeeze again to squirt drops out of the pipette.

Squirt - Flood - Squirt - Flood!

I practically had to issue flood warning and gave him a fresh sheet of kitchen towel every 5 minutes or so… ^_^”

And of course, more colors for him to explore… :)

Bursting with Color

And while we were at it, it wouldn’t hurt to do the classic erupting volcano experiment as well, would it? ;)

Bubble, Bubble.. See the Volcano!
