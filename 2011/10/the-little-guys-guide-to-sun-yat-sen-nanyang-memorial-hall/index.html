The Little Guy's Guide to Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall

Oct 31, 2011

Yesterday, despite the huge pile of laundry waiting to be ironed, we finally decided to explore the “uncharted waters” of Balestier.

The Little Guide

Not the entire Balestier, of course. The weather was just not that friendly… We picked Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall, especially since yesterday was the last day of the free entry promotion… :)

Sun Yat Sen Nanyang Memorial Hall

Gee… what were we thinking?! Of course, the place was jam-packed! To begin with, the museum isn’t that big… and yeah, we should never have underestimated people’s interest in museum, especially on a weekend AND with that “free-entry” gimmick.

It was nearly impossible to take a picture inside the hall from a good distance without including any passerby… >_< I lost interest in using my camera even before we finished the first gallery.

Judging from the number of times we went to a museum in a year, you should be able to tell that I’m pretty much a museum person. Yet, I felt kinda lost inside. Somehow it was simply hard to make up the whole story behind the memorial hall just by looking at the displays and reading the notes. Thankfully, a guided tour started not long after we reached there. At least, we went home with a little story to share should anybody ask us about the place and the history… :P

But, nope… No way I’m going to write the story here… I’ve done enough essays back in my school days… :P
