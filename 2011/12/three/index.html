Three

Dec 3, 2011

One day, as we built a tunnel from a MegaBloks set…

The Little Guy: Mommy, take some cars… (asking me to get some cars from outside, so that he could play with them and the tunnel). I’m tired…

Mommy: No, no… Mommy has just built the tunnel. Mommy’s tired, too…

The Little Guy: No, Mommy. I’m tired three…

Mommy: _speechless_…

Today, as we bid farewell to the world of the Terrible Two, we welcome the Trying Three. His days of being our one sweet, little angel may soon be outnumbered by the many days of power struggle, defiant behaviour and talking back.

But, no worries… just bring ‘em on! ;) We’re ready!
