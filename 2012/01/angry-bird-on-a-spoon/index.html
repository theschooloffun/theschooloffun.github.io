Angry Bird on a Spoon!

Jan 31, 2012

Back in my younger days, marbles on teaspoons was one of my favorite games. The game is such a no-brainer - all you have to do is to run or walk while balancing the marble in the bowl of the spoon… Yet, it proves to be fun for both the players and the audience.

Charlie and Lola’s I’m Really, Really, Really Concentrating book certainly brings back those memories (Hey, there’s the TV series, too!). I thought I should share the game with the little guy and he agreed to it - and I’m just glad he didn’t insist on using an egg, like Lola. It really helps that the game is such a no-brainer, too… :P

I’ve got the start line ready in no time. The little guy, too, seemed to have his own idea of a start line, using a book we borrowed from the library… :)

The Start Line

The only problem, though, is that we don’t have any marbles at home. Yup, I’m still waiting for the coast to clear (read: nobody would swallow those colorful marbles). I know… I think I’ve been waiting for too long… >_<

Anyway… so along came the angry bird :) A special angry bird made from a ping-pong ball by my husband at a recent birthday party.

Whohoo.. let the game begin… (^o^)/

His Perfect Egg

The Finish Line!

The little guy certainly did have a great time! And he even learned how to pronounce “concentrate”, just like Lola.
