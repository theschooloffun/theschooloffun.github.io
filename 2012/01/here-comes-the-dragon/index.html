Here Comes the Dragon!

Jan 20, 2012

So… Chinese New Year is next week…

We certainly don’t want to let it pass without making anything New Year-ish. We’ve been seeing so many cute dragon pictures everywhere we go, so it wasn’t that hard to figure out what to make… :)

What we had:

  1. paper (we use red, yellow and orange)

  2. black paint (we use watercolor paint)

  3. big brush

  4. glue

  5. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

I thought red would be the most appropriate color for the festive season, so I prepared small pieces of red paper for the little guy to paint on.

Chinese Painting in Progress

I told him to draw an S like a snake in each piece and here’s what we got…

S-S-S-Snake...?!

Frankly, it was a bad move to ask him to “draw an S like a snake”, because later on he kept insisting that they were snakes, not dragons… :P I still think they look more like dragons, especially with the big heads which he “accidentally” painted each time… hee hee…

We left the paintings dry overnight and the paintings were ready for some decoration the following day… :)

Next, the little guy helped me cut some small triangles while I made some small stars. We then used these triangles and stars to decorate our dragons.

Twinkle, Twinkle Little Stars

And here’s what we got… :)

Star-Studded Dragon

Puff, the Scaled Dragon
