The Treasure Box

Feb 21, 2012

It is often said that one man’s trash is another man’s treasure. Yet, these days, I’ve come to realize that I should also add another: one man’s trash is the same man’s treasure.

I’ve been trying to do mini “spring-cleaning” every now and then. Each time, I would collect a bag full of things to throw. But, of course, like always, I let it sit in one corner for a little while more before I finally decide to throw it away.

Sometimes, I find myself changing my mind: I decide to keep some items for yet another unknown period of time. The reason? Well, they turn out to be rather… uhm, useful.

Like this box of loose old sequins and beads. One day, the little guy just decided to entertain himself by burying a key chain ring and later on digging in to find it.

Where's the Ring, Mommy?

There It is!

He played this game for a good 15 minutes, so that box is definitely here to stay… :P It’s just the perfect kind of game when I don’t feel like running around after him.

Sometimes, I decide to re-purpose the items before they really make their way into the dustbin.

Like old newspaper and used toothbrush…

Practising Spray Painting

.. and used red envelopes…

Cut!

And Stick!

They are just wonderful to entertain this busy little guy.

There is, however, one “flaw” in this system of mine… >_<

I realize that each time, this means I would just have to add yet another item to my pile of things to treasure…and this one is definitely not going to make it into the dustbin anytime soon!

To Keep.. (For Now!)

Aaarghhh… This is totally not a proper spring cleaning!!!
