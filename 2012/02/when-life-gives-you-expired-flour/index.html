When Life Gives You (Expired) Flour...

Feb 6, 2012

More often than not, being a stay/work-at-home mom means you’ll get a higher chance of having to deal with your child’s mood swing, or worse, tantrum, within those precious 24 hours. It’s just the “lovely” perks… :P

Sometimes, it could be just a short episode. So short we could hardly remember to count at the end of the day.

Sometimes, the storm of emotion just drives us nuts. Seriously, those times would be when I wish we could just play my favorite game: hide-and-sleep. I hide, while the little guy sleeps. Or vice versa: he hides while I sleep. I am pretty sure I could do both hide and sleep well, if only he could deactivate his superpower in finding his mommy… ^_^”

Oh well… until we finally find a chance to try out this hide-and-sleep game, I guess I would have to resort to this game of (expired) flour to lighten up his mood. I know, it’s super messy. Yet, the mess that we’ll have to deal with later on is really nothing compared to those tantrum episodes.

It is as easy as pouring some flour into a big bowl.
