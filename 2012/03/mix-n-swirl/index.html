Mix 'n Swirl

Mar 9, 2012

The kitchen has finally got to see the line of food coloring bottles waking up from its slumber. I know, it has indeed been ages since we last used any food coloring. It wasn’t that the little guy hadn’t been nudging me enough. In fact, in the past weeks, he had been begging me to let him play with those bottles each time he spent his time long enough in the kitchen to notice those bottles… >_< It’s just that I’m not in the mood of cleaning up the colorful mess. Really. That was all.

It was a different story, of course, when one Saturday, my husband offered to entertain the little guy with those bottles…_grinz_ I was pretty sure he would clean up the mess, so I was all “Yes! Here, you can take all the bottles!” Hee hee…

So, the two of them happily turned themselves into a bunch of water scientists, mixing colors and different kinds of liquids.
