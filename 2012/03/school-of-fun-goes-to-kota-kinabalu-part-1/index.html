School of Fun Goes to Kota Kinabalu! (Part 1)

Mar 21, 2012

Nine years, people!

The Wait

Nine years and ten months, to be exact. That is how long I waited for this trip.

The Destination

Back then, I was merely a student, with unbridled energy and enthusiasm for all the things that attract people to this small part of the world called Sabah: mountain climbing, trekking, diving, snorkeling, and rafting. Yeah.. I was that unstoppable. Except for one thing: tight budget… ^_^”

Last week, a week after my dear husband announced the pleasant surprise he had for me (i.e. a 4-day trip to Kota Kinabalu.. yayy!), there I was… finally setting foot on the wilderness of Sabah, Malaysia. Yet, due to very foreseen circumstances, my to-do list shrank significantly. Forget about mountain climbing and trekking… on our second day, I could already foresee that the only mountain I would see in this trip would be that mountain of laundry at the end of the trip.

Nevertheless, we had a whale of a time there. Wait for more stories ahead … ;)
