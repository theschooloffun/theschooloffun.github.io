School of Fun Goes to Kota Kinabalu! (Part 3)

Mar 23, 2012

Finally, the highlight of our trip - island hopping!… :) (uhm, at least, for this round)

Picture Perfect Beach

On our third day, we took a speed boat to Tunku Abdul Rahman Marine Park. The Marine Park offers 5 islands, but we decided to just visit 2 of them - Pulau Sapi and Pulau Manukan.

The two islands look pretty much alike from a natural perspective - tiny, fringed by blinding white sands and surrounded by crystal sea. My husband and I took turn to snorkel trying to make the most of this trip.

Here, Fishy Fishy Fishy...

The Rainbow Fish Look-Alike

You see, most of the time, we were among this crowd… enjoying a dip into the sea.

Enjoying A Dip in the Sea

The little guy, however, thought the beach was much more fun.

Exploring the Sand

He dig in sand, sift it, pour it, build with it… enjoying the feel and smell of it.

The Sand Cupcake

Yes, he loves playing with sand.

Yet, somehow, during this trip, he developed a certain dislike for the rolling waves that crashed along the beach… >_<  It got even worse when my husband came back with cuts and scrapes from sharp-edged barnacles. The little guy claimed that the fish bit his daddy! And that was it… He refused to go anywhere near the water!

This was the closest he got…

The Clingy, Screaming Beach Boy
