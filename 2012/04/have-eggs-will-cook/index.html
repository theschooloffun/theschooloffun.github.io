Have Eggs, Will Cook

Apr 12, 2012

Don’t get me wrong.

I love our new kitchen. I do want to make sure it is durable and have no intention to prove how much abuse it could withstand.

Yet, as much as I want to keep it spick and span, everyone in the family seems to want to try using it… >_< Yup, including the little guy.

Apparently, he got tired of his toy kitchen. And everything that mommy does in the kitchen always looks … oh-more-interesting-than-anything-else!

So, this morning, he begged me to let him cook egg. Till now, I couldn’t figure out why I said yes in the first place… :S Just think of how much adult supervision I need to put to ensure that everything stays intact! Eeeksss… and to think that the new kitchen is not even a month old!

Obviously, accidents are bound to happen. Even during the first step: breaking the egg into a bowl.

<a href="/uploads/2012/04/298_egg_01.jpg" title="I Thought You Said "Table", not "Bowl"" target="_blank"><img src="/uploads/2012/04/298_egg_01.jpg" alt="I Thought You Said "Table", not "Bowl""/>

I Thought You Said "Table", not "Bowl"

The good news is the little guy has been observant enough to remember exactly how I usually break eggs. The bad news is he just doesn’t like to wait. He refused to wait until I put down my camera and help him with the egg. My heart sank as I watched how the egg spilled all over the table and floor… ^_^”

Oh well, at least he did manage to put a teeny-weeny drop of egg into the bowl.

We took another egg. This time round, I made sure I held his hands as he broke it into the bowl.

He proceeded with the whisking part while I heated the frying pan.

Whisk ... Whisk

He seemed to be more careful now that he knows how sticky eggs are… :P

And finally, it was time to add the beaten egg into the frying pan… his favorite part! :) If only you could see how excited he got as he scraped the egg from side to side… :P

Again, Mommy!

And hey… he actually listened to me and took the basic safety precaution not to touch the hot surfaces.. :)

To end this entry on a sweet note: The kitchen survives… yayyy… :) And somebody ate a hearty breakfast this morning… ;)

So, will we do this again next time? Uhmmm… let me think about it… ^_^”
