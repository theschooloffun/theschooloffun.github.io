A Puzzling Thought

May 4, 2012

Children are fascinating.

It’s just hard to figure out what’s in the mind of a child. Like why the little guy, of all the places, had to insist to place his glass full of juice on a remote control.

Maybe he wanted to learn about the physics of balance. Maybe he wanted to see what happen if the glass tipped and fell.

To Fall or Not to Fall

I don’t know.

I just wanted to make sure nothing was spilled.

Or how these days the hot weather could easily spoil his mood… yet how, a minute later, playing peek-a-book under piles of pillows (which made me perspire like mad!) could send him into giggle fit.

I also wonder what has happened to my little clean freak… :S

Here Come the Fingers!

Look, Mommy, Two Hands!

He used to be reluctant to plunge into anything messy. But now… he seems to enjoy getting his hands dirty!

Yes, children _are _fascinating!
