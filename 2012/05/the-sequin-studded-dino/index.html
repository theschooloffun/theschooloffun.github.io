The Sequin-Studded Dino

May 10, 2012

Last week was a rather low key week for all of us. The little guy was down with fever for 2 days. Getting out of bed seemed like a big chore for him, so he stayed in bed for pretty much the whole day.

Thankfully, his fever went down on the third day… :) His energy level swung up sooner than I expected, though. And yup, being cooped up all day with such an energy is simply frustrating for him!

After a while, he relented and decided to ransack my crafts drawer instead. And once again, the sequins saved the day! ;)

The Sequin-Studded Dino

His energy level might be somewhere up there, but apparently, his impatience level hadn’t returned yet… :P Carefully, he glued the sequin, one by one! Geee…

Next, I gave him some tea leaves to add to his dino.

Sprinkle, sprinkle

And that was it! An instant craft activity… :)
