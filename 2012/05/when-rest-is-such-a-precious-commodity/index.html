When Rest is Such a Precious Commodity

May 28, 2012

It’s amazing how much one can achieve in a day.

Sadly, the same goes for how much a toddler can watch in a day… :S

No doubt, the recent erratic weather has taken its toll on our health. The messages (and the signals from my body) received so far have been pretty clear: cut some slack and get some much needed rest. Yes, right…r-e-s-t. That’s exactly what I need. But then, it also means that it gets tougher to keep the little guy away from the screen… >_< Yup, any screen… mobile phones, TV, computer, or even camera.

Surely there are times when the little guy is such a wonderful companion. He could choose what he wants to play, help himself with all the props and stick to the same thing for an hour or so, good enough for me to get some rest. Obviously, in his very own little world, he could turn a stool into a tunnel, a mailbox into a door and a wash basin lego piece into a fridge! Yup, it’s very entertaining to watch… :P

Creating His Own Lego Scene

Yet, most of the time, he still needs that “little nudge” from his mommy to stay away from any screen. You know, like  showing him how to appreciate observing what would happen when you try mixing water and oil.

Water and Oil

We probably have done this a thousand times. Still, it seems like it worked wonders to distract him from watching yet another show on TV… :P

Just add some food coloring and a pipette for more fun…

Burst of Color

Whee… it just got better… :D

Water Droplets

Let him continue shaking, pouring and adding more drops to his heart’s content, and yup, plenty of rest, here I come… ^o^ I just need to remember not to close my eyes for too long and to get a cloth ready for him to clean up the mess… :P

And while waiting, why not explore the water beads, too? ;)

Before

(Puff!) After

Ahh… the bliss! Now if you could just excuse me, I’ll go and grab my pillow…^_^
