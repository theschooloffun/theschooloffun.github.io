Pin the Dino!

Jun 25, 2012

It must not have been easy to be a big sister or brother. Things just, well, change. I now find spending one-on-one time with the big brother has become a luxury we can’t always afford. The fact that the big brother doesn’t show much jealousy sometimes makes me a little worried, too. What if he actually is jealous now that I can’t spend much time with him? What if he’s been hiding his feelings?

Mountain-and-Valley Drawing for Dino

We’ve been trying our best, though, to keep things for the big brother the same as they were before the little brother arrived, as much as possible.  … :) Thanks to all the help we’ve been receiving, we could still steal some time every now and then to make something together. Just like in the past. I really hope we could stick to this.

Just the other day, when we could finally say goodbye to the syringe that the big brother used to take his flu medicine for the past few weeks, we found the syringe to be such a great tool to paint our dinosaur during our playtime together (read: while the little brother was sound asleep).

Syringe Painting

A Polka-Dot Dino

And when we have cut out the dino, pinching the clothes pegs onto the dino turns it into a cute stegosaurus! And hey.. putting numbers on the clothes pegs also turns it into a great avenue to learn counting, too… :)

A Polka-Dot Stegosaurus!

I hope this little thing goes a long way in helping the big brother adjust to the new baby… :)
