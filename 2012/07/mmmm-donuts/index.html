Mmmm... Donuts!

Jul 13, 2012

I must admit that a good support system is crucial as we adjust to life with a newborn baby. It will maintain the sanity of not only a new mother, but also a mother of a newborn AND a toddler teeming with energy… Like me… :D

My mom, for example, seems to have a great way in keeping the big brother’s outbursts at bay…most of, if not all, the time… :P

Like one day when she got the otherwise grumpy big brother busy making his favorite donuts.

Getting His Hands Dirty!

Really, Dirty...

That moment, I almost couldn’t recognize that patient and hardworking toddler!

Dusting with flour

He waited patiently (read: went to take his nap) as my mother worked her magic to turn the dough into golden-brown donuts.

Mmm....yum yum

Then, he worked hard with the chocolate rice, trying his best not to put some into his mouth throughout the process… :P

Dab.. dab...

Yet, surely, when it was time to eat… he was once again back to his real, cheeky, self…:P

Munch! Munch!
