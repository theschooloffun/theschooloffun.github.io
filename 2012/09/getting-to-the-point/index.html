Getting to the Point

Sep 12, 2012

One fine morning, after much struggle, I actually managed to lay out an invitation to paint.

Come and behold!

It came really handy especially as the little brother morphed himself into a very hungry caterpillar for the day (again).

To my surprise, the big brother seemed to enjoy using those cotton buds, instead of the usual paint brushes. He carefully used different cotton bud for different color and did all the color mixing on paper, not in the palette.

Paint Pointing

Pointillism, Anyone?

We started off with a piece of paper and 2 different colors. Yet, we ended up with more than 10 colorful artworks! It was indeed amazing that this once restless boy could sit still and paint for an hour or so and still ask for more later in the afternoon.
