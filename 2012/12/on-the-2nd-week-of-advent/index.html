On the 2nd Week of Advent...

Dec 13, 2012

Now that we’ve got the stable ready, it’s time for Joseph to make his way… :)

What we had:

  1. Paint (we used acrylic)

  2. water (to dilute the paint)

  3. egg cartons

  4. pipe cleaner

  5. black marker

  6. paint brush

  7. scissors

Let the Fun Begin!

I started off by cutting some random shapes out of the egg cartons.

Three

Basically, the idea was to get the big brother to stack them up into one little egg carton man.

Plain

Next, he painted. He chose red for the outfit, so red it shall be… :P I know, stylo-milo!

Red

I helped him draw the eyes and mouth.

Hi, Joseph!

Notice the little black bib under the chin? Well, I thought Joseph would need a beard, so I got the big brother to stick a small, black piece of egg carton. It ends up looking more like a scarf, though.. ^_^”

We then cut and twist a yellow pipe cleaner. Yup, a staff for Joseph… :)

Ready to Lead
