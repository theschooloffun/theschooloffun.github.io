A New Found Freedom

Jan 2, 2013

02.01.2013.

That date shall go down in history. We’re officially parents of a baby AND a school going kiddo now!

Of course, with this new found freedom (Oh … a glorious 4 hours! Could you believe that?!) comes our very first date this new year. I know, we should have sent him to school earlier… >:P Yet, with the sudden “silence”, it felt like we were given a preview of how life would be when the kids spread their wings and fly on their own… _sigh_

As much as I enjoy having a non-kid meal, I do miss sharing a plate with that cheeky boy of ours. As much as I love having a great (real!) conversation just the two of us without any unnecessary interruption, we do miss all his silly remarks.

And guess what? Suddenly, I didn’t mind seeing the toy strewn living room when we got home. Or those bread crumbs under the dining table.

I guess I wouldn’t mind his non-stop chatter the moment he gets home either! >_<

Wow.. it really is great to miss those kids, isn’t it? ;) Yikes… Absence really does make the heart grow fonder!
