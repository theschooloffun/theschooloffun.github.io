First Week of School: The Aftermath

Jan 4, 2013

Getting Ready

Three days into the new school term and I must say that this whole thing will soon do no good for my waist >< *_gasp*

You see, Ive been eitheron a dating spree or on a feasting spree. Both involved indulging myself and eating whatever I want to eat. Aww think of all the things I could eat without having the big brother spying on my food consumption! Yup, the list is endless and resistance is futile bwahaha >:P

Joining the Feast

Oh wait youre right. Theres still the little brother. I guess, for now, hell make sure I dont lose my waist _grinz_ Hes been such an excellent companion both in merry-making (Naptime?! What naptime?) and in making sure I dont get drown in excessive me time (Me time? I thought its supposed to be you-and-me time, mommy!) ^_^

Anyway, the new school term indeed has started off a new rhythm in our lives. But, wow hasnt it been great! The big brother is super duper excited about his school and his excitement really rubs off on all of us. Its contagious, I tell ya! He makes me feel like going back to school, too!

I know, this might just be a fleeting phase. His I want to go to school! Now! remark first thing in the morning may not last till next week. Two weeks from now, I may be the one rushing him to go to school, instead of him begging me to change to his school uniform one hour before school starts. He would probably drag his feet instead of telling all the passers-by that hes going to school in the way hes been doing these past three days. So, let me just bask in this excitement. And oh, that big, slobbery smooch in front of the classroom? That is also ALL mine! Well, at least until the day he finds it intrusive :P
