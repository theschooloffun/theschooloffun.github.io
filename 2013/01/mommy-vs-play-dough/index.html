Mommy vs. Play Dough

Jan 11, 2013

Wanna guess where the little brother’s favorite spot in the house is?

Next to his big brother.

His favorite game?

Grab and munch. Everything in sight.

And his big brother’s current favorite toy?

Playdoh!

Gimme That!

Eeekss.. >_<

Now that the little brother is becoming more and more mobile, one of his favorite pastimes includes putting mommy’s lightning speed to the test.

Sure, Playdoh is nontoxic. But, doesn’t the box say “Fun to play with, not to eat”? Also, it is certainly no fun not knowing what goes into the little brother’s tummy when he gulps one big piece of Playdoh by chance. (Don’t worry… The score so far shows 1:0 for super mommy vs. eaten Playdoh…_grinz_)

And so began our search for an edible, homemade play dough. There are plenty of play dough recipes if you care to google it. I have yet to find our most favorite, though. Otherwise, I would have shared it here… ;)

Anyway, just for fun, I added some peppermint extract. It smelled great at first, but the smell wore off after a while. I should probably add more next time… ;)

I didn’t get to add any food coloring. Obviously, the sous-chef (read: the big brother) could not wait for that extra step… _sigh_
