What Big Brothers are For

May 24, 2013

Very soon, the Big Brother would celebrate his first anniversary of being a big brother. I’ve never been one, but I’m pretty sure it’s not easy to be a big brother.

You see, as a big brother, you’re responsible to teach your minion everything under the sun, such as how to brush your teeth…

Tooth Brushing 101

… or how to operate that weird-looking toy…

Spin It Like You Mean It

.. or climb….

Come, Higher!

and even how to speak fish.

In Aquarium, Speak Like a Fish

In addition, you’ve got to make sure that your minion is ready for the real world. First, you have to remember only to share the right stuff. Definitely a rocket science for a 4-year-old! Just when you thought it’s good to share those cookies that you would never have thought of sharing with anybody before, your mom screamed in panic. So much for thinking to do a good deed… tsk … tsk..

Only Share the Right Stuff

And when conflict arises, you’ve got to remember all those best practices in negotiation skill fast, apply them and make sure your minion get them, too. Yup, especially when yummy cookies are at stake.

The Famous Sharp Stare

Yeah… you’ve got to take care of your little brother, people often say. But exactly how, they’ve never said.

Kissing a boo boo? Piece of cake!

Bye, Boo Boo!

Wiping a snotty nose? Eekss… gross! But, will do…

-snort-

Once in a while, you get to share The Squeeze. But only with the certain appropriate level of strength… whatever that is…

Squeeze Attack!!!

Ridiculous, I know. Isn’t it only when you’ve experienced such squeeze that you would appreciate more… uhm, well, the joy of not being squeezed?

What else?

He’s got trouble falling asleep?

Go to Sleep, Little Baby

Don’t worry, big brother is here to stay.

See, I told you, not easy. At all.

It even requires a lifelong commitment!

If that first anniversary doesn’t call for celebration, I don’t know what does…:P
