Our Not-a-Box!

Aug 16, 2013

It took me all the courage in the whole world, but it was worth it… :)

You Found Me!

Geee… it was even tougher than answering when my husband asked if I would be more excited getting a diamond ring or a box… :P

One question, and it was all ours to keep. All three of us happily did the winning dance as we lugged the cardboard box home from our neighbor’s place. Yup, even the little brother! Aren’t we glad our neighbor bought a new aircond!!!

It was the biggest box we could ever think of getting. And with it come new and exciting possibilities. The two boys have been entertaining themselves with various games, inside and outside the box.

Feel like playing, too? Go on … get a box for yourself and have fun! ;) Don’t worry about not knowing what to do with the box. Antoinette Portis’ Not a Box will provide you with tons of ideas… ;)
