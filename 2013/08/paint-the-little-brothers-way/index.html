Paint the Little Brother's Way

Aug 28, 2013

There was a time when arts and crafts activities with the big brother meant that the little brother was fast asleep.

Or simply somewhere away from the big brother.

Let me go out, too...

Away from the mess.

Away from any potential hazard… :P

Not long after came a time when we finally let the little brother hold his own brush. No paint, just water.

Brushing 101

How he wielded his brush like a pro!

It took us a while before we mustered enough courage to let him use paint. And, on his first debut, the little brother chose to paint with… the wrong end of the brush… :P

Do a drum roll!

And finally… the right side of the brush… :P

Bingo!

Since then, there is no turning back. When the big brother is busy with his paint, the little brother, too, will get busy.

All Fingers Unite!

How about their mommy, you ask? Oh… she’ll get really, really busy… >_<

Obviously, somebody likes to paint his body more than anything else _gasps_.
