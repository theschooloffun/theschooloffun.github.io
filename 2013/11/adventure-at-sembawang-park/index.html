Adventure at Sembawang Park

Nov 28, 2013

Is it just me, or does this holiday really go fast? Too fast for our liking!

Anyway, after some eenie meenie minie mo, we decided to pack our lunch (read: buy takeaways… that’s how you got ready in less than half an hour… :P) and set sail to Sembawang Park.

Go on.. I'll watch the space

Nobody was interested in the “picnic”. Sand vs Food 1:0 At the beach, we don’t eat food. We dig, dig, and dig some more… That’s the rule.

The Digging Crew

We do some serious ship watching. Or whatever that thing is called…

Look!

See… even that one digs…

Working Hard

But the real gem was something that we nearly missed. This wasn’t part of the initial plan. But who in his right mind would ever want to miss this shipwreck playground?!

Ship Ahoy!

Just between you and me, it’s actually a battleship playground. Yet, the big brother is all about pirates these days. And on the ground, it does look more like a shipwreck… :P

Climb aboard!

The playground is intended for children 6 to 12 years of age. So, you can imagine that the little brother wasn’t that happy, although the big brother found the challenges exciting.

What?! Five more years?!

There are also some fireman’s poles… another treat for the little fireman enthusiast.

Yo, Hooooo!

The weather might be scorching hot (how else would you explain the clear blue sky in all the photos!?), but we were glad we made it to the park… :D

My only regret? I think I’ve just accidentally raised the bar. All the other playgrounds now seem to  pale in comparison… >_<
