Into the Unknown

Nov 20, 2013

Mommy: Where are you going today?
__The Big Brother: We’re going to Timbuktu!

Get Ready!

Okie… Timbuktu sounds very far, even when we have almost 2 months of holiday to spend…

The next best alternative?

For once, the big brother got to choose his own travel destination and mode of transport. And oh, he got to choose where to start, too… :)

He decided to take bus. He told me which bus he had randomly chosen.

That One!

Uhm… he actually chose the first bus in the first berth from the first bus interchange. So, probably it wasn’t that random after all… hee hee… :P

We then proceeded to check the bus route from the leaflets.

This One

Guess what, the bus he chose is a loop bus! It runs on a single route that starts and ends at the bus interchange. A short route! So, we certainly could afford to sit from end to end… yipee! (^o^)/

And so, we boarded the bus and had an adventure together. Each bus_ _stop displays the 5-digit bus stop number, which is also shown on the leaflet. The big brother happily matched the bus stop numbers and most of the landmarks (the 3-digit apartment block numbers or the building names). Not bad for some number recognition practice… :P

At the end of our trip, I got 2 boys smiling from ear to ear and all ready for lunch. So, I guess it was really worth the trip!

Try it yourself! ;)
