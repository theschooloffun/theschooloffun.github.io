Make Your Plan and Laugh

Nov 24, 2013

Ask us about our holiday plan and we’ll tell you a list of things that we want to do.

Not an exact plan, but all the things that we really hope to achieve by the end of this holiday.

So what's the plan?

The truth is, life is full of other little details that may bog you down, or rather… bog mommy down… :P

And when mommy is too busy to fully entertain you, aren’t we all thankful for siblings!

Faster! Faster!

Sure, I bet you’ve heard this before: If you want to make God laugh, tell Him about your plans. But, really, maybe we should try laughing with Him, too… and simply enjoy that fleeting moment :)
