Patience: Training in Progress

Nov 7, 2013

I used to think I’m a very patient person.

Along came the 2 brothers…

Two

and I thought I have just got myself two of the most impatient people in the whole universe… :S

Picture this…

“Moooommy! Can you help me find my fire engine?”

“Baaaa…!” translation: I want mommy!”

“Waaa…!” translation: Mommy, carry me!”

“Mommy, where’s my fire engine?”

“Mooooommy, come here. Look here. Quuiiiiick!”

“Keeeeeeees… peeeeeeessss….” translation: keys, please”

“I want the keys, too. Me! Me!”

“Peeeeeessss….”

– inspired by a real life event–

Oh yeah, all in a day’s work.

There are days when I feel like it’s been such a loooong day and yet it is only 9 o’ clock. In the morning!!! _gasp_

Then, it suddenly hits me. Maybe I’m not that patient after all. Maybe I’m actually the one__ who is impatient here. When they ask me for something, they don’t actually mind waiting (oh well, as long as it’s not that long!). Sure, they’ll whine. But that’s okay. It’s usually me who gets to lose my patience first by thinking that they want it immediately while I am busy with something else. And oh yeah, toddlers cry, whether or not you give in anyway…. :P

It also turns out that these 2 kids have been generous enough to train my patience, pulling me in all directions. They seem to conspire to teach me not to sweat the small stuff.

While one looks forward to be the first to reach a certain destination, the other takes his own sweet time exploring all the little holes along the way. While one does not care about slowing down, the other simply doesn’t bother being left behind.

Yayy! Hole!

I could run after one and rush the other one, or I could stay back and bark at the other one to slow down.

Or… I could simply just walk at my own pace. Let them enjoy themselves.. while I enjoy myself, too … :) As long as everybody’s safe… :)

And oh, speaking about holes, if your child loves holes as much as my little ones, why not try Herve Tullet’s The Finger Circus Game?

The Finger Circus Game

It’s just the perfect book to entertain any hole lovers… :P

The Perfect Fingers!
