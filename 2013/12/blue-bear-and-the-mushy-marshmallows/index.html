Blue Bear and the Mushy Marshmallows

Dec 21, 2013

Christmas is drawing nigh. And how will we deal with it?

We can approach it frantically, trying to strike out each and every thing in our list.

On the Table

Or we can… keep calm, sit down and enjoy…

Mushy!

There will be marshmallow squashed on the table (and somebody trying to lick it off… >_<).

Somebody will eventually spill some hot chocolate, leaving stains (hopefully, not permanent!) on your favorite tablecloth.

There will be annoying sticky marshmallows on those small T-shirts.

But that’s okay… stay calm.

Yumm-o!

It’s time to make memories that last a lifetime… :)
