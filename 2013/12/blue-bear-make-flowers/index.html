Blue Bear: Make Flowers

Dec 21, 2013

Check out what Blue Bear was found with last Thursday!

Be Mine...

Yup, making flowers we would… simply because cutting felt scraps is therapeutic. Especially for the hyped-up in this season of rush… :P

What we had:

  1. Felt scraps

  2. Scissors

  3. Chopsticks

  4. String

Let the Fun Begin!

This is one great way to get rid of those felt scraps. The big brother helped to cut into smaller strips. No, it doesn’t need to be perfectly of the same size. Imperfection is beauty… :)

Tied up with a string

Once you’ve got them all tied up with a string, get a chopstick to poke through your pom pom, like a stem.

Make one. Or two. As many as you like. With as many colors you could think of.

Put them in a jar, if you like.

In a Jar

Sit down and admire… or stand…

Look Great from Above
