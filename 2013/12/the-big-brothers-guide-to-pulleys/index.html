The Big Brother's Guide to Pulleys

Dec 9, 2013

Just because the big brother is the only one going to school these days it doesn’t mean that the rest of the gang isn’t picking anything new from the school.

For the last school term, his school chose Simple Machines as its theme. The kids were introduced to various device, from wheel and axle, inclined plane, wedge, to pulley.

And so, the big brother taught us how to make a simple pulley… :)

What we had:

  1. Toilet paper roll

  2. Chopsticks

  3. String/Yarn

  4. Masking tape

  5. Old CDs

  6. Bottle cap

Let the Fun Begin!

First, we needed to make the wheel. We attached 2 CDs to a short toilet paper roll using masking tape. The toilet paper roll would be where our string sits on. Next, we put a chopstick through the center of the toilet paper roll so that we could rest our pulley on our house’s gate… :)

For the load, we used a milk bottle cap.
