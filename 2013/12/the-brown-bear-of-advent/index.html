The Brown Bear of Advent

Dec 2, 2013

This Advent season, doing an advent calendar sounds very tempting. Yet, seriously, this holiday, I’ve begun to doubt my planning skill… :P You see, doing an advent calendar requires planning out the activities way beforehand. And yup, making that advent calendar sounds like a daunting task when you’d rather use the time for something else… >_< The last time I planned to watch a show with the big brother (what?! It was last week?!), they were already cleaning the stage by the time we reached… :S

And so, instead, Inspired by Eric Carle’s Brown Bear, Brown Bear, What do You See, we decided to put our little brown bear in the limelight… :) The idea is, each morning, the big brother gets to do his current favorite game: treasure hunt. Once he finds the brown bear, he’ll get to guess what we will do that day as a family.. :)

There. So much easier to “plan”… ;)

We were lucky that we’ve got something planned out for us on the first day … ;)
