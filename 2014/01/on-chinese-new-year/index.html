On Chinese New Year

Jan 31, 2014

It’s Chinese New Year and frankly, none of us here is a real expert in the celebration. Every year, I’m just glad to learn that we should be putting our brooms and brushes away. No home cleaning on New Year. Great. I’m relieved from all my housekeeping duty!

But, hey, maybe this New Year, we should go beyond that. So, we went to borrow our “manual” from the library.

Source: Goodreads

The big brother said he likes this story about a family preparing for the New Year: the Chinese decoration, dumplings, firecrackers and the like. Sounds like a great manual? There is only one problem, though: it’s already Chinese New Year! Oh well, let’s just say we’ve got ourselves the manual to prepare for the next Chinese New Year… Yup, we’re that excited and early… ;)
