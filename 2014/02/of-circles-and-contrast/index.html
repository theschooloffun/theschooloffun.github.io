Of Circles and Contrast

Feb 2, 2014

As the big brother and I explore contrast - one of the principles in art - we learned about Sonia Delaunay. We looked at one of her works, Electric Prisms, and thought that we should made something similar to call our own… :)

We began with a treasure hunt to find various objects that are circles around the house, such as glass, coaster, coins, etc. and drew our different-sized circles.

Circles and More

Next, we colored our circles. The big brother chose oil pastel and so we used the oil pastel to learn about different shades for the contrast.

Contrast!
